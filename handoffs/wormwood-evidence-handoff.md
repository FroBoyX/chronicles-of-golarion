- Erastus 5, 4712 AR: wake aboard the Wormwood.
- Five press-ganged new hands: Mirei, Harvus, Rak, Sho, Conchobhar.
- Crimson "Cog" Cogward volunteered, was part of early trials, but was not press-ganged.
- Day 1 includes Plugg trials, work assignments, Jakes Magpie keelhauling, Sandara returning Mirei's harp, and first-night social beats.
- Day 2 includes Scourge proxy harassment, new hands defeating Aretta / Fipps / Jape / Syl, harassers whipped for lateness, and several preserved social scenes.
- Sho never drank rum rations; she dumped or hid them and was not caught, unless later evidence says otherwise.

- All four harassers ended unconscious and were whipped for lateness.

- Sho tried to bargain with Grok using future usefulness and failed because she had no concrete value.

- Rak returned to Grok that night and offered to brew alcohol; this is the first recovered explicit friendship-development beat with Grok, not necessarily first contact.
