# Rak Player Narrative Beat Questions

## Document Status

- Type: Player-facing short question list
- Maturity: Draft
- Recipient: Marshall / Rak's player
- Purpose: High-value relationship and motive beats only

## Sendable Version

Marshall, we have Rak's sheet and the old logs, so we do not need a full rebuild from you. This is just to jog the important relationship and motive beats so we can preserve Rak's truth rather than only his mechanics.

Short answers are fine. No need to remember exact rules.

## Rak's Core

1. What did Rak want most when he first came to Port Peril: glory, freedom, a real pirate career, family legacy, chaos, or something else?
2. How seriously did Rak take his infamous Free Captain ancestor? Was that a burden, a boast, a destiny, or mostly a joke?
3. Was Rak's devotion to Besmara sincere faith, pirate culture, superstition, convenience, or some mix?
4. The sheet gives Rak both **Omen** and **Trustworthy**. Did you see him as someone people should be able to trust, someone unsettling, or both?

## Grok and Kroop

5. What was Rak's relationship with Grok really like: casual, romantic, strategic, funny, sincere, or some mixture?
6. Did the goodbye with Grok feel like a real emotional beat for Rak, or just a fun shipboard moment?
7. If Rak later learns Grok suffered because of Harrigan or the Wormwood, how hard does that hit him?
8. What did Kroop mean to Rak: mentor, drinking buddy, boss, friend, burden, or shipboard family?

## The Other PCs

9. Did Rak consider Harvus, Sho, and Mirei his crew by the Bonewrack fork, or just people he had survived with?
10. What does Rak think of Sho judging his violence during the Man's Promise boarding?
11. Does Rak have a personal line about killing surrendered, unconscious, or helpless enemies?
12. What does Rak think of Harvus after Harvus lost the +1 returning spear but used it to save Sandara and kill the Whale?
13. Does Rak want repayment for the spear, credit in the story, an apology, or is the debt mostly a joke?
14. What does Rak think of Mirei after everything with Plugg and the cave rescue?

## Friends, Enemies, and Loose Grudges

15. Your sheet lists Grok, Ambrose, Badger, Tilly, and Sandara as helpful. Which of those relationships actually mattered emotionally to Rak?
16. What made Jack only "Friendly?" instead of clearly trusted?
17. Why were Patch and Tate/Tam only indifferent rather than enemies?
18. Why is Maheem on Rak's "kill if a moment arises" list?
19. Is Slippery Syl just an enemy, or does Rak respect her competence at all?
20. Did Shivikah helping Rak during the frog fight change how he sees her?

## Bonewrack and the Cold Open

21. At the fork, what is Rak's immediate priority: survive Syl, get supplies, find Harvus/Mirei, rescue Sho, return to the ship, or something else?
22. If Rak sees Harvus, Mirei, Sandara, Tilly, and Goyle alive, what is his first instinct?
23. Does Rak understand that the Bonewrack rescue could become a legend, or is he focused on practical survival?
24. What does Rak think the group should do about Plugg and Scourge if they get the upper hand?

## Ship Future

25. If the Man's Promise becomes available, does Rak want to take it, leave it, sell it, rename it, or see who can actually hold it?
26. Would Rak accept a captain, a vote, a council, or rule-by-whoever-is-strong-enough?
27. What role would Rak actually want on a free ship: captain, quartermaster, cook, alchemist/surgeon, enforcer, chaos agent, or something else?
28. What would make Rak stay with the others after Book One?
29. What would make Rak walk away?

## One Missing Thing

30. What is the biggest thing about Rak that sheets and logs do not capture?
