# Harvus Player Narrative Beat Questions

## Document Status

- Type: Player-facing short question list
- Maturity: Draft, updated with Sho-facing rage accountability follow-up targets and storm-memory follow-up
- Recipient: Harvus' player
- Purpose: High-value relationship, motive, and memory beats only

## Sendable Version

We have Harvus' light timeline and the current archive, so this is not a full rebuild request. This is meant to jog the important relationship and motive beats so we preserve Harvus' truth rather than only the event sequence.

Short answers are fine. Memory fragments are useful.

## Harvus' Core

1. Before the Formidably Maid, how did Harvus see himself in Port Peril: laborer, exile, survivor, thug, protector, outsider, or something else?
2. How hostile was Harvus toward humans at the start of the campaign, and what softened or complicated that hatred?
3. What did Harvus lose before the campaign that still mattered to him: family, home, village, dignity, safety, or something else?
4. Was Harvus' connection to Calistria mainly faith, revenge, comfort, habit, or a private bargain?
5. How did Harvus understand revenge: sudden blood, patient payback, poetic justice, or something more personal?

## Press-Ganging and Scourge

6. What did the wing injury mean to Harvus beyond pain: humiliation, fear, disability, rage, violation, or proof that Scourge had made it personal?
7. Did Harvus immediately remember Aretta, Jape, and Maheem as press-gang attackers, or did that realization grow over time?
8. When did Harvus decide Scourge had to die?
9. Was Harvus trying to control his rage aboard the Wormwood, or did he believe rage was the only honest answer?

## Wormwood Relationships

10. What did Sandara mean to Harvus at first: healer, beauty, kindness, ally, priestess, possible love, or something else?
11. When did Harvus' interest in Sandara become emotionally serious rather than shipboard attraction?
12. What did Grok becoming helpful mean to Harvus: practical access, real trust, manipulation, friendship, or mutual respect?
13. What did Rosie mean to Harvus after he recovered her fiddle?
14. How did Rosie's loss at sea affect him?
15. What do you remember about Harvus influencing Samms, Ratline, Conchobhar, and Badger?
16. Were any of those social wins emotionally meaningful, or mostly survival work?

## Sho, Rak, and Mirei

17. What did Harvus think of Sho before the Man's Promise boarding?
18. What changed when Sho told him Scourge tried to have her killed and had her boxed?
19. What did Harvus think of Rak during the Wormwood and early Bonewrack period: dangerous, useful, crew, rival, funny, reckless, or something else?
20. What did it mean when Rak gave Harvus Sandara's holy symbol before the island search?
21. What did Harvus think of Mirei before Bonewrack?
22. Did Mirei coming back into Riptide Cove with Harvus change how he saw her?

## Violence and Crew

23. During the Man's Promise boarding, did Harvus feel guilt, satisfaction, fear, rage, duty, or something else about the people he killed?
24. How did Harvus react to Sho being disturbed by the killing?
25. When Harvus says or believes "we are crew," what does that mean to him?
26. Does Harvus believe crew is chosen, earned, imposed by survival, or created by shared danger?

## Rage, Accountability, and Restraint

27. Looking back on the Man's Promise boarding, does Harvus believe his anger hurt or killed anyone who did not deserve it?
28. If Sho challenged Harvus about rage causing collateral damage, would he feel accused, ashamed, angry, defensive, or understood?
29. Would Harvus ever vow to aim his rage more carefully, or would that feel like giving up the only weapon the Wormwood left him?

## Storm, Bonewrack, and the Rescue

30. What do you remember Harvus doing during the storm before Sandara, Tilly, and Goyle's abductions became clear?
31. What was Harvus' immediate emotional state when he found Sandara's hat in Riptide Cove?
32. After the first failed cave assault, did Harvus expect to survive going back?
33. When Harvus returned with Mirei, was the goal rescue, revenge, proving something, or all of it together?
34. What does Harvus remember about cutting Tilly, Goyle, and Sandara free?
35. What did it mean to give Tilly the flail?
36. Did Harvus know he was creating a legend in the Cauldron, or was he only trying to get people out alive?
37. How does Harvus feel about losing Rak's spear?
38. Does Harvus understand Goyle as a rescued man, a witness, a potential ally, or a future problem?

## Future Pressure

39. If Harvus gets back to the Man's Promise, what comes first: Sandara's safety, Scourge's blood, Plugg's fall, keeping people alive, or seizing the ship?
40. Would Harvus accept another captain, a council, a vote, or rule by whoever proves strongest?
41. What would make Harvus stay with the others after Book One?
42. What would make Harvus walk away?

## One Missing Thing

43. What is the biggest thing about Harvus that sheets, notes, and logs do not capture?
