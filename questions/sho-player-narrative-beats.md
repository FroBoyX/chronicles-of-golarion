# Sho Player Narrative Beat Questions

## Document Status

- Type: Player-facing short question list
- Maturity: Draft
- Recipient: Sho's player
- Purpose: High-value relationship, motive, and memory beats only

## Sendable Version

We have Sho's sheet notes, the old logs, and the current archive, so this is not a full rebuild request. This is meant to jog the important relationship and motive beats so we preserve Sho's truth rather than only her mechanics.

Short answers are fine. Memory fragments are useful.

## Sho's Core

1. What did Sho want when she left Jalmeray: freedom, knowledge, discipline on her own terms, discovery, Agatha, ruins, or something else?
2. How did Sho feel about her monastery by the time she left: grateful, trapped, angry, loyal, misunderstood, or mixed?
3. What did Agatha Dreamriver mean to Sho: friend, patron, rescuer, romantic anchor, proof of kindness, or something else?
4. Did Sho actively worship Irori, or was Irori more background/monastic culture/sheet artifact?
5. What drew Sho to the Ghol-Gan map lead: history, mystery, ambition, escape, duty, or personal curiosity?

## Press-Ganging and Wormwood Defiance

6. What did Sho remember about the fake contact/Kipper and the drugged meeting at the Formidably Maid?
7. When Sho woke aboard the Wormwood, was her first reaction fear, anger, calculation, shame, discipline, or something else?
8. Did Sho see the Wormwood as a place to survive quietly, resist openly, or judge until the right moment?
9. What was Sho's line between discipline and obedience?
10. What did Sho think of Plugg and Scourge early, before things became openly lethal?

## Owlbear, Aretta, and Early Moral Pressure

11. What did the Owlbear fight mean to Sho?
12. Did refusing to kill Owlbear feel like mercy, discipline, defiance, or a statement about strength?
13. What does Sho remember about the bilges conflict with Aretta?
14. How responsible does Sho feel for Aretta's death?
15. Was Aretta's death a mistake, a tragedy, justice, unavoidable shipboard violence, or something else?

## Watch List and Crew Memory

16. Your notes mention possible foul play with Giffer's body. What did Sho suspect happened?
17. Your notes say Maheem/Muheem, Fipps, Jaundiced, and Slippery were people to keep a serious eye on. What had each done to earn that caution?
18. Your notes say Crimson and Ratline were people Sho had things to discuss with. Who was Crimson?
19. What did Sho need to discuss with Crimson?
20. What did Sho need to discuss with Ratline?
21. Did Sho have any unexpected allies or almost-allies aboard the Wormwood?

## Harrigan, Peppery, and Authority

22. What did Sho think of Captain Harrigan before the Man's Promise boarding?
23. Why did Sho warn Harrigan during the boarding fight?
24. Did Harrigan's recognition or reward change how Sho saw him?
25. What did Sho think of Peppery Longfarthing: warning sign, authority figure, attraction, danger, respect, or something else?
26. Did Sho believe there could be honorable authority aboard a pirate ship?

## The Other PCs

27. What did Sho think of Harvus before the Man's Promise boarding?
28. What did Sho think of Harvus' rage after hearing Scourge tried to have her killed or boxed?
29. What did Sho think of Rak before the boarding battle?
30. What exactly disturbed Sho about Rak's violence?
31. Did Sho believe Rak was dangerous to enemies only, or dangerous to the crew too?
32. What did Sho think of Mirei during the Wormwood period?
33. Did Sho notice Mirei becoming closed off or drawn into officer attention before the crisis became clear?

## Man's Promise Moral Fallout

34. Your notes say Sho was disappointed and saddened by the conversation with the crew they took over. What conversation was that?
35. What did Sho mean by failing to stop her boarding crew from slaughtering people?
36. Did Sho feel responsible for the Man's Promise dead as an attacker, as someone who failed to lead, or both?
37. Your notes preserve Nor'Gal and dead brother Cor'Wal. What does Sho remember about them?
38. What did Goyle being former quartermaster mean to Sho?
39. Does Sho still have or remember the Rahadoumi captain's amulet?
40. What was the 1100 gp sack from Riaris, and what did Sho intend to do with it?

## Hot-Box and Kahina

41. What does Sho remember immediately before waking in the hot-box?
42. What did Kahina feel like to Sho: enemy, judge, ally, executioner, mirror, or test?
43. When Sho offered to kill Plugg and Scourge, was that repentance, survival, justice, rage, or all of it?
44. Would Sho actually kill for repentance?
45. What would make Sho hesitate?
46. You mentioned a later or additional memory of being under deck with a monk-like character telling Sho to get ready. Was that Kahina, another Man's Promise survivor, or someone else?
47. Was that scene about freeing Sho, preparing for mutiny, or preparing for Plugg/Scourge to act?

## Future Pressure

48. What does Sho believe justice requires for the Man's Promise dead?
49. Would Sho accept a pirate crew if it had rules, restraint, and accountability?
50. What would make Sho stay with the others after Book One?
51. What would make Sho walk away?
52. Does Sho still intend to reach Magnimar or return to Agatha someday?

## One Missing Thing

53. What is the biggest thing about Sho that sheets, notes, and logs do not capture?
