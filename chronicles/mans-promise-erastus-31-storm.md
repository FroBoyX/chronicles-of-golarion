## GM-Locked Short Form

On Erastus 31, 4712 AR, a large sudden storm blasted the Man's Promise.

All hands were needed just to keep her afloat.

The ship was tossed by waves and driven southwest off course.

During the storm, grindylows attacked the ship.

The two sailors taken in the initial storm abduction were:

- Tilly Brackett
- Sandara Quinn

The GM followed the AP storm encounter almost beat-for-beat for this section: Tilly and Sandara were taken first, then the grindylows attacked openly.

## First Preserved Combat Fragment

The currently preserved chat-log fragment begins after all hands have already been called on deck and while the fight is already underway.

Visible initiative values include:

- Harvus G'ron: 24
- Mirei: 21
- Rak Wraithraiser: 8

The combat occurs on the `Man's Promise` map.

### Rak Under Attack

Rak takes repeated damage in the visible fragment, dropping as low as 2 / 20 HP before receiving healing back to 9 / 20 and later 17 / 20.

Sho later says she and Rak fought "a bunch of grindilows" on deck, and that the grindylows seemed especially interested in Rak.

The fragment does not explain why the grindylows focused on Rak.

### Harvus in the Rigging

Harvus is in the rigging during at least part of the fight.

He later asks Sho what happened on deck because he felt something pierce the sails while he was up in the rigging.

Sho answers that it must be where a grindylow's head went.

The GM note attached to the fragment says this seems to refer to the head of a grindylow being thrown or launched into the sails.

The fragment does not yet establish who launched it.

### Mirei Falls, Sho Cushions Her

Mirei falls from the rigging during the storm.

Sho sees the danger, runs roughly to where Mirei is going to land, and cushions / catches the fall enough that Mirei gets up and thanks her.

Sho is visibly relieved afterward.

The fragment does not establish the exact mechanics, damage, or whether Sho prevented all injury.

## What This Establishes

- The open grindylow fight happened during the storm on the Man's Promise map.
- The PCs visible in this fragment are Harvus, Mirei, Sho, and Rak.
- Harvus was up in the rigging during part of the fight.
- Sho and Rak fought grindylows on deck.
- Rak took heavy damage and was bloodied during the fight.
- Mirei fell from the rigging in the storm.
- Sho physically intervened to cushion / catch Mirei's fall.
- Sho and Mirei share a small but direct rescue beat in the storm.
- The grindylows appeared especially interested in Rak, but the cause remains unresolved.
