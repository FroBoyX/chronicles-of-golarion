## Narrative Log

The night shift began under Fipps Chumlett's voice.

He called Harvus first.

> "Bird man."

Harvus answered, and Fipps sent him to the mainsail with "the dark one." Then Fipps turned to Sho.

> "Monkey.... repairs."

Sho clenched her fist, but obeyed.

> "Aye...."

While they worked, Sho tried to reach across the silence toward Goyle.

> "So.... Goyle was it?"

Goyle answered, but Jaundiced Jape glared over silently. That was enough to end the moment.

Sho looked toward Jape, sighed, and let the conversation drop.

> "Oh... Another time...."

Goyle accepted it.

> "As you wish."

By morning, Harvus had nothing useful to report. Sandara asked whether he had noticed anything. He had not.

> "Afraid not milady."

Sandara told him to keep an eye out. Harvus kissed her hand and headed for bed. Sandara beamed and promised to keep him posted.

In the captain's cabin, Plugg woke Mirei into the name he had demanded the night before.

> "You're awake... have you made a name wench?"

Mirei offered the first shape of it.

> "Mistress sounds a lot better to me."

Plugg's eyes narrowed. He asked whether the crew would simply call her Mistress.

Mirei clarified.

> "Mistress Mirei."

Plugg accepted the persona, but made it an order.

> "You must live into that persona... go, interact with the crew. Scourge will be watching you. Prove you deserve this honor... and can truly be the voice of a pirate lord."

He sent her into the dead captain's things.

> "Find clothing befitting this new station from the captains clothing."

Then he added symbols of his own authority.

> "There is a jewleled sword in the storage as well, strap ware it as a sign of my authority... and jewelry."

Mirei dressed herself in the former captain's clothing, jewelry, and a jeweled sword. Plugg's new symbol of station was now visible.

At dinner, Kroop saw her coming.

> "Look... is't mistress...."

Plugg had ordered a captain's grand meal for her as well.

Sho noticed the dress at once and whispered:

> "Where did you get that dress?"

Mirei could only answer in passing.

> "Its a long story. I can't right now."

Sho accepted it for later.

Mirei brought Plugg his food. He ordered her to taste it, as he had made part of her duties. She tasted the first plate, then the second. Both seemed safe.

Plugg told her the third plate was hers, but she would not be eating yet.

> "Scourge and I have a meeting about the days... activities..."

He sent her back among the crew.

> "Come back after you go back out amoung the crew, talk to them, gauge where they stand and report back to me 'Mistress'."

Her food would wait.

Below decks, Sandara greeted Harvus warmly, then saw Mirei and paused.

> "...Mistress?"

She looked around the room and then bowed into the performance.

> "To what do we owe this honor?"

Mirei said she was just coming down to be around people. It was nice to be out among the crew.

Sho was confused and concerned.

> "Are things alright Mirei?"

Then, more quietly:

> "What's going on?"

Mirei gave the simplest truth she could.

> "I seem to have become the captains 'mistress'."

She gestured toward the clothes and sword.

Sho stared wide-eyed, then recovered herself enough to whisper.

> "You mean like that Chalk woman? Are you being forced? Or is something else going on?"

Mirei answered carefully.

> "Not really forced... Something else. I can't get into it right now though."

Sho nodded.

> "Just talk to me when you need me."

While that conversation happened, Sandara used her closeness with Harvus as cover. She hugged him, joked, laughed too loudly, and whispered in his ear.

She needed him to use his shift.

> "I need you to see if you can do something on shift... sneak into eather the Captains or officers quarters on shift."
>
> "See if you can find information about where we are going."

She needed confirmation.

> "I need to know if we are headed to Bloodcove, or the area."

Harvus warned that the quarters would probably be locked, and that he had no skill with locks, but he would see what he could do.

Sandara also considered another route: Sho.

> "What of Sho, you two have worked together, I'm not sure we can trust her... tact."

Harvus answered that Sho put herself in odd places with the crew, but handled herself well in a brawl.

Sandara considered whether they could use that: convince Sho to do it the rough way and get the information. But she knew the cost.

> "We'd be risking her life though."

Before Harvus left, Sandara kissed his cheek. Harvus kissed her lips for luck. Sandara let him and sent him on.

Mirei continued the task Plugg had given her. She approached Tam "Narwhal" Tate and tried to make conversation.

Tam recognized the new station with contempt.

> "Look who's graced us with her voice?"

Mirei smiled and asked how the fine night was treating them.

Tam answered bluntly.

> "Fine, what do you want?"

Mirei said she was looking for conversation to pass the time and wanted to see how they were doing.

Tam set the terms.

> "Look.... just because I've been told to respect you, doesn't mean you don't have to earn it..."
>
> "Permission to be frank Mistress?"

Mirei gave permission.

Tam was merciless.

> "You seem out of place in your station... we, are pirates... I don't know what Plugg sees in you but this is a pirate ship, and frankly, if you want to inspire the crew or even talk to us... your going to have to have a little more character 'little girl'. Go back to your gilded cage."

Mirei visibly frowned but held the line. She had given permission to be frank, so she would let the gilded cage comment go. She said she hoped to earn the respect of the crew.

Tam laughed that hope leads to Davy Jones. If their brat of a captain saw her as his voice, Tam hoped they all ended there sooner rather than later.

Then he made it personal.

> "I personally put you on the Wormwood 'Mistress'... with all 'due' respect, don't make promises you can't keep."

Tam told her she could report him to Plugg if she wanted. It would only prove cowardice and inability. He had worked on the Wormwood for years; she was a green swab, or shark bait.

> "Quit while you're ahead."

Then he bowed into the title.

> "If I'm being frank, of course. 'Mistress.'"

Fipps called for the next shift.

Mirei did not take Tam's bait.

> "I don't plan on being a snitch, Frank, so I wont be running to tell him about this. Enjoy the rest of your night."

Then she left.
