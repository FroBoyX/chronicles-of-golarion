**Erastus 16, 4712 AR**

Approximate voyage mapping: Wormwood Day 12 if Day 1 is Erastus 5.

## Setup — Land Ho

Sho woke slumped and tied to the mast.

Master Scourge was there, placing the whip under her chin as she came around.

> "Oh good.... You're awake."

He asked whether she had enjoyed it.

Sho groaned, then answered the grin on his face.

> "Im guessing you did from that grin on your face"

Scourge did not hide it.

> "Yes... yes I did."

Before the exchange could fully continue, Ratline's voice rang out from the rigging.

> "Land ho~!"

The call stirred the deck into commotion.

Sho asked to be let down if Scourge would allow it. Scourge said Plugg wanted to talk to her and released her chin, needling her with the line that a tough girl like her could handle it.

Then the summons widened.

Scourge ordered the bloody lizard brought on deck.

Plugg called Harvus out of the rigging.

Rak arrived in his usual voice.

> "Sup brah"

Scourge unlocked Sho and told Rak he was due up there too.

Rak seized on the word.

> "DUE!?"

Sho rubbed her wrists and stood wearily.

Elsewhere, Tam "Narwhal" Tate delivered Mirei's summons.

> "You're wanted on deck... that voice of yours doesn't get you out of everything."

Mirei headed up.

A few moments later, Plugg stood with buckets at his feet.

Four had been gathered: Harvus, Rak, Sho, and Mirei.

Plugg then yelled for Scourge to get Badger.

When Badger was added, Plugg pointed the five toward the reef to the fore and starboard.

> "Good, good. The captain wants crabs... and I know just the lot to get them!"

Mirei sighed.

Sho sighed with her.

Rak treated it as surf.

Plugg gave the task plainly. They were to swim to the reef, fill each bucket with crabs, and swim back.

> "Am I 'clear."

Then came the order to drop anchor and get to work.

The five jumped from the Wormwood and began swimming toward the reef, leading into the reefclaw encounter.

## AP Scaffold Context

The AP scaffold for this scene sends the PCs and one friendly NPC to a reef to fill crab pots for Harrigan's supper. The reef is dangerous because reefclaws live there and attack after the group reaches the coral.

Reefclaw meat counts as enough to fill a crab pot. Bringing reefclaw meat back for Harrigan can trigger the AP reward: Harrigan returns one PC's confiscated equipment, or gives a potion if the character's gear has already been recovered.

The table version follows that reward shape with Rak.

## Return to the Ship

The crew returned with their catches one at a time.

Plugg judged Mirei's catch first.

> "That's what you brought.... they look awfly small."

Mirei muttered that they looked fine to her and went to take them below.

Plugg approved Rak's catch more strongly.

> "Hmm, good. Good. Talk to fishguts and cook them up for captain."

> "Those make fine eating."

Harvus returned with a bucket of crabs. Plugg insulted the catch as small and blamed Harvus' nature.

> "Small, I suppose it's to be expected from a bird in the water... to the gally."

In the galley, Mirei delivered her crabs with less ceremony.

> "TAKE MEH CRABS"

Kroop recognized the danger of what they had found.

> "You ran into these bastards out there huh? Can be ruff in the reefs. Were they any trouble?"

Mirei said they were nasty and had tried to eat her legs.

Rak claimed he had dropped one immediately.

> "No way brah, 1 shot that like a beast"

Sho came back later, still feeling the previous night's punishment.

Plugg asked what had taken her so long.

Sho answered plainly.

> "Hard to do some swimming after a night shackled to the mast."

She still had a full bucket.

Plugg turned the delay back into discipline.

> "Guess you'll have to mind that pretty mouth... to the gally."

## Rak Before Harrigan

After the cooking, Caulky Tarroon came to the galley. The captain wanted to know who had caught the big one.

Rak identified himself.

Kroop confirmed it.

> "Afraid it's true lass, it's my assistance."

Caulky was not pleased that it had to be Rak, but she took him and the dinner to Harrigan.

She introduced him as the source of dinner.

Rak greeted the cabin with his usual lack of ceremony.

> "Sup bros"

Harrigan accepted the result.

> "You've done well, I've instructed Grok to give you anything we took from you from our stores... remember, I smile on those who go above and beyond!"

Rak took that as good news.

Scourge sneered.

Riaris Krine wanted the food brought on and asked whether the reefclaw had been feisty.

Rak described the fight in his own way, and Caulky nearly choked at the phrasing while serving.

Riaris did not care about the strange delivery.

> "Good enough for me! Dig in."

Harrigan reinforced the lesson for Rak.

> "Do right by me lizard and I'll treat you right!"

> "Theirs money in it for you, and infamy too!"

Scourge glared.

Plugg dismissed Rak with a rum warning.

> "You... are dismissed, don't let me catch you throwing that rum out."

Scourge muttered that he hoped for the chance.

Peppery Longfarthing observed the obvious.

> "What a strange creature."

Riaris reduced the matter to the practical outcome.

> "Caught good food, good enough for me."
