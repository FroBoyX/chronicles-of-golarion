## Purpose

Use this file as the compact reference for the Man's Promise / Bonewrack crew-state problem.

This file corrects the older drift where the eight unnamed Man's Promise survivors were left in an unresolved Arodus 5 actor bucket. It also clarifies the ship-crew requirement used when deciding whether the Man's Promise can be sailed after a mutiny or faction split.

## Rules Reference — Sailing Ship Crew Requirement

The Man's Promise should be treated as a Pathfinder First Edition sailing ship for crew-viability purposes.

Working reference:

- Pathfinder First Edition sailing ship vehicle statistics list **Crew 20**.
- In this archive, **20 crew** means the effective operating crew needed to sail the ship properly and sustainably.
- This does **not** mean the ship cannot be moved at all with fewer than twenty people in an emergency, but below that level the ship should be treated as under-crewed, slow, unsafe, unable to maintain watches properly, and vulnerable to failed repairs, bad weather, combat, fatigue, and morale collapse.

Campaign application:

- A post-mutiny victory does not automatically mean the PCs can sail away.
- The ship needs enough willing or controllable people to cover rigging, swab work, repairs, bilge/water control, helm/watch, cooking, and command acceptance.
- The current crew-state question is therefore not just **who wins the fight**, but **who remains alive, aboard, capable, and willing to work afterward**.

## Corrected Erastus 27 Ship-Split Model

The ship split on **Erastus 27, 4712 AR**.

Correct sequence:

1. The Wormwood captured the Man's Promise.
2. The post-battle captured Man's Promise survivor lineup contained **15 captured survivors**.
3. Captain Harrigan made an example of one weakened captured sailor by throwing that sailor overboard.
4. **14 captured Man's Promise survivors** remained alive after that execution.
5. Harrigan promoted Mr. Plugg to captain of the Man's Promise and assigned him a skeleton crew.
6. The Wormwood and the Man's Promise separated on Erastus 27.
7. Of the fourteen surviving captured Man's Promise crew, **six named survivors** were assigned to Plugg's skeleton crew.
8. The other **eight surviving captured Man's Promise crew went with the Wormwood**.
