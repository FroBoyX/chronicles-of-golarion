## Rules Reference — Man's Promise Crew Requirement

The Man's Promise is treated as a Pathfinder First Edition sailing ship for operating-crew purposes.

Working reference:

- PF1 sailing ship vehicle statistics use **Crew 20**.
- In this archive, Crew 20 means the effective operating crew needed to sail the ship properly and sustainably.
- Fewer than twenty people may still move or reposition the ship in an emergency, but the vessel should be treated as under-crewed, unsafe, and vulnerable to failed repairs, poor watches, fatigue, bad weather, and morale collapse.

## Man's Promise

### Status

The Man's Promise is damaged from the reef incident but appears to be moving or preparing to move along the Bonewrack shore by the time Sho speaks with Kahina.

Kahina tells Sho that the ship has set sail for the far shore to take on supplies, then will leave for an unknown destination.

## Crew Number Constraint

The Man's Promise requires enough sailors to operate.

Use **Crew 20** as the effective-operations target for a PF1 sailing ship like the Man's Promise.

If too many people die, desert, refuse service, remain captured, or split into factions, no side may have enough effective crew to simply sail away.

This is a pressure tool, not a railroad.
