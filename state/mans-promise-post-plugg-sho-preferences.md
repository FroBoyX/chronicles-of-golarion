## Current-State Warning

This document does **not** establish that Plugg has lost command.

This document does **not** establish that the Man's Promise becomes an anti-pirate ship.

This document records Sho's likely preferences if a post-Plugg ship-politics decision emerges.

## Sho's Immediate Goal

Sho does not intend to remain in pirate life.

If Plugg is removed and the ship can be controlled, Sho's immediate hope is that the Man's Promise reaches a town or major port where she and anyone willing can escape the pirate existence thrust upon them.

## Possible Lofty Future

Sho can imagine a lofty future where the Man's Promise becomes an anti-pirate vessel: a ship that travels the seas taking out pirates without belonging to a navy.

The moral aim would be to prevent future versions of what happened to the Man's Promise.

This is aspirational, not a settled plan.

## Command Preferences

Sho might trust **Kahina Bukekayo** in command.

Sho might trust **Sandara Quinn** in command if Sandara makes it back.

Other candidates would depend on who remains after the crisis.

Sho would definitely not trust **Harvus G'ron** or **Rak Wraithraiser** in command.

Sho does not think **Mirei** is cut out for command.

Sho does not think she herself is worthy of command.

## Rules Sho Would Insist On

Sho would insist on:

- no killing innocent people,
- taking only what is needed,
- not leaving people without enough to survive and reach port,
- and treating those rules as temporary survival limits until the group can get to port, not as a long-term pirate code.

This is partly a reaction to the Man's Promise boarding and the conduct of Rak and Harvus.

## Voice in Decisions

Sho would want a voice in what happens aboard the ship.

Her reason is not ambition. She wants to make sure people do not fall out of line and resort to cruelty.

## Likelihood of Staying

Sho already does not plan to stay aboard long-term.

She would likely leave once the ship reached a major port town.

A larger reason would be needed for her to stay:

- a larger threat she felt responsible to handle,
- or someone she needed to save.

## Use in Future Play

Use this file if the table reaches a decision point after Plugg is removed, incapacitated, overthrown, or otherwise loses control.

Until then, treat it as Sho's future-facing preference, not a current plan.
