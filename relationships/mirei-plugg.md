# Mirei and Mr. Plugg

## Document Status

- Type: Relationship / coercion record
- Maturity: Draft, seeded from Man's Promise first-night and Erastus 29 scenes
- Scope: Plugg's use of Mirei as entertainer, cabin-bound asset, renamed symbol, and coerced tool
- Source basis: Direct chat logs and GM reconstruction
- Spoiler scope: Character-limited / GM-facing until revealed in play

## Summary

Mr. Plugg's relationship with Mirei is not romance, patronage, or simple favoritism.

It is coercive possession wrapped in advancement language.

Plugg recognizes Mirei's performance skill and tries to convert it into shipboard control: entertainment, morale, status, proximity, spying, loyalty proof, and eventually a new identity attached to his imagined rise as a pirate lord.

## First Man's Promise Night

On the first recovered night aboard the Man's Promise, Scourge ordered Mirei to report to Plugg with her instrument.

Mirei performed with a borrowed fiddle and impressed Plugg.

Plugg offered her a hammock in his cabin area, saying someone with her skill should not be down with the crew below.

When she accepted because it would be quieter, the offer immediately became coercive. Plugg performed a slow weapons check, pushed her against the bulkhead, took a pouch, allowed her to keep a potion, and promised that her life would improve if she kept him entertained.

Afterward, he ordered her not to touch anything without permission, to play for him each night, and not to speak to him or Owlbear unless spoken to.

This established the pattern: privilege as isolation, protection as ownership, and performance as service.

## Erastus 29 Morning Duties

On Erastus 29, Plugg expanded Mirei's role.

When she brought him food, he quickly folded a chart, then ordered her to taste both dishes. After she did so, he declared food-tasting part of her duties.

When Scourge called her "wench" and Mirei objected, Plugg appeared to defend her dignity, then struck her and corrected the phrase to "Captain's wench."

This showed that Plugg would control not only what she did, but how humiliation and respect were defined around her.

## Erastus 29 Night Agreement

That night, Scourge opened the cabin door and greeted Mirei as "Captain's wench," then left.

Plugg asked what the night's entertainment would be. Mirei chose the fiddle again. Plugg accepted it and told her to prepare a song for the next day.

After the performance, Plugg returned to the "unpleasant" wench business.

He framed the next step as agreement: if Mirei agreed to be his entertainer, she would stay near him, stop working as a swab, and be treated with the respect of an officer on the Man's Promise.

Mirei hesitated. She said she would not mind still helping around the ship because otherwise she feared the respect would be sarcastic and fake. She also said she liked entertaining.

Plugg rejected labor as the path to respect. He told her she would earn respect with her voice and presence, that he would raise her in station with him as a lady of a pirate lord, and that she must sing his deeds.

Then his voice went cold and he demanded an answer.

Mirei agreed.

Plugg then defined the terms:

- only he would call her wench;
- everyone else needed a name for her, and she had to provide that name by morning;
- the former female captain's clothing and belongings were now hers;
- he would not tolerate disobedience;
- if she did his bidding, she would earn the respect of the crew and those who heard of Plugg's exploits;
- if she did anything wrong, he would give her to Scourge to do with as he pleased;
- until then, she had Plugg's protection.

This is the clearest recovered statement of Plugg's offer / threat structure.

## Relationship Meaning

Plugg is trying to turn Mirei into a symbol of his claimed legitimacy.

He has taken the captain's cabin. He has taken the former captain's space, clothing, and belongings. He now tries to assign Mirei into that conquered space as singer, ornament, status marker, and controlled officer-adjacent figure.

The new-name demand matters because Plugg is not only trying to change what Mirei does. He is trying to rename how others see her.
