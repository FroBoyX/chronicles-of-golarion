# Rak Wraithraiser and Cut-Throat Grok

## Document Status

- Type: Relationship record
- Maturity: GM-confirmed seed
- Scope: Wormwood period before capture of the Man's Promise and prize-crew split

## Summary

Before the capture of the Man's Promise and the later splitting of the Wormwood crew, Rak Wraithraiser and Cut-Throat Grok hooked up.

Afterward, Rak began bunking with Grok.

This is a major relationship beat and should be treated as campaign canon.

## Campaign Significance

This changes Rak's Wormwood social position.

Rak was not only Kroop's cook's mate and an increasingly visible PC; he also developed a personal relationship with the Wormwood quartermaster.

Because Grok controlled the quartermaster's store and was Kroop's closest friend, this relationship places Rak close to two of the most important non-officer support figures aboard the Wormwood.

## Timing

- Occurred before the capture of the Man's Promise.
- Occurred before the prize crew was split from the Wormwood.
- Occurred while Rak was still aboard the Wormwood.
- Grok was aware that Rak was being transferred to the Man's Promise.
- Rak and Grok shared a sentimental goodbye before the ships separated.

## Separation

When Plugg chose the Man's Promise prize crew, Rak was separated from Grok.

Grok remained aboard the Wormwood.

Their goodbye was sentimental, not merely logistical.

If Grok's story continues along the later published Adventure Path direction, this goodbye may become a grim plot point.

## Future Function

The relationship can matter later if:

- Rak returns to the Wormwood,
- Grok learns of the Man's Promise outcome,
- Harrigan's vengeance brings Wormwood figures back into play,
- Kroop invokes Grok as trusted support,
- Grok's later AP fate is used or adapted,
