# Harvus G'ron and Rosie Cusswell

## Document Status

- Type: Relationship record
- Maturity: Draft
- Scope: Wormwood fiddle recovery, friendship, and storm loss

## Summary

Harvus G'ron and Rosie Cusswell developed an early friendship aboard the Wormwood after Harvus helped recover Rosie's fiddle.

Rosie's later loss during the storm gives this relationship tragic weight.

## Fiddle Recovery

Harvus' light timeline records that he recovered:

- his ring,
- his necklace,
- and Rosie's fiddle.

Recovering the fiddle was a direct way of helping Rosie regain something personal after being press-ganged.

## Friendship

Harvus' notes later record that he made friends with Rosie.

This should be treated as a meaningful influence success unless contradicted by formal records.

## Rosie's Loss

Rosie was lost at sea during the Wormwood storm despite rescue efforts.

Because Harvus had already helped her and become her friend, this death should not be recorded as only an environmental casualty.

It is an early loss in Harvus' Book One emotional history.

## Campaign Significance

Rosie's death comes before Sandara's capture and rescue.

That sequence matters:

1. Harvus helps Rosie.
2. Harvus loses Rosie to the sea.
3. Harvus seeks solace with Sandara.
4. Harvus later refuses to lose Sandara the same way.

This may help explain the intensity of his response to Sandara's disappearance.
