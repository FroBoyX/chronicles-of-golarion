# Harvus G'ron and Sandara Quinn

## Document Status

- Type: Relationship record
- Maturity: Draft, expanded from Harvus light timeline evidence and Day 3 dinner scene
- Scope: Wormwood through Bonewrack rescue

## Summary

Harvus G'ron and Sandara Quinn form one of the central emotional relationships of Book One.

The relationship begins aboard the Wormwood through Sandara's early aid, Harvus' attraction and courtly reverence, and shared shipboard vulnerability.

It becomes defining on Bonewrack Isle when Harvus returns to Riptide Cove to rescue Sandara.

## Early Wormwood

Sandara helped the PCs understand shipboard survival, Bloody Hour, and Grok.

Harvus identified her as a healer and was immediately attracted to her.

On Erastus 7 / Wormwood Day 3, Sandara invited Harvus to sit with her during dinner in the middle hold and asked whether he was adjusting to the ship.

Harvus answered with courtly respect and asked to know more about her: where she was from and how she came to be aboard.

Sandara hesitated, saying she did not like to go into it. Harvus said he would not push if it was sensitive.

Because he respected that boundary, Sandara shared part of her history: she was raised in Hell Harbor, grew up mostly on the port streets because her parents were overworked, worked on the docks when she could, got into trouble, found taverns by nine, and was at sea by twelve.

Harvus told her his heart was filled with joy at the chance to meet her, then left to rest his wing and kissed her hand.

Sandara invited him to ask more later and checked whether resting the wing was helping.

This is the first recovered extended Harvus/Sandara dinner conversation and establishes the courtly tone of Harvus' affection alongside Sandara's willingness to share more when not pressed.

## Comfort and Care

After Rosie's loss, Harvus sought solace with Sandara.

After a dangerous reef encounter, Sandara healed Harvus.

Harvus' notes also record drinking heavily with Sandara and passing out.

These moments show the relationship moving beyond formal gratitude into emotional reliance.

## Man's Promise

After the Man's Promise boarding, Harvus spent time aiding Sandara's recovery.

Harvus' notes later record a kiss or smooch with Sandara during the Man's Promise phase.

This confirms the relationship had moved into explicitly romantic territory before Bonewrack.

## Bonewrack

When Sandara went missing, Harvus searched the deck with Rak.

Rak later gave Harvus Sandara's holy symbol of Besmara before the island search.

That object made Sandara's danger tangible.

Harvus' priority on Bonewrack became finding Sandara.

## Riptide Cove

Harvus first entered Riptide Cove with the group and nearly died.

After recovering, he returned with Mirei rather than abandoning Sandara.

In the Cauldron, he saw Sandara sinking and treated her rescue as the mission.

He cut captives free, carried Sandara upward, killed the Whale with Rak's spear, and escaped with Sandara and Tilly above water.

## Campaign Significance

This relationship changes Harvus' role in the campaign.

He is not only a wounded avenger.

He is someone who went back into the dark for another person.

Sandara's survival is therefore one of the clearest reasons Harvus can become legendary without becoming merely violent.

The Day 3 dinner scene also shows that Harvus' affection was not only dramatic rescue energy. It began with patient curiosity, restraint around Sandara's boundaries, and concern for her story.

## Current State

- Sandara is alive, rescued, and returning with Harvus.
- Harvus has just risked death to save her.
- Sandara likely still has Besmara's tricorne.
- Harvus may still have or may need to return Sandara's holy symbol.
- The relationship's next scene occurs under immediate political danger, not peace.
