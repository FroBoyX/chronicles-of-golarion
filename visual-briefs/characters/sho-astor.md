## Character Identity Cues

- Suli / Jalmeray visual cues should be quiet and grounded: disciplined posture, restrained monk clothing, hints of elemental heritage through skin/eye coloration rather than flashy elemental effects.
- Strength under restraint is central.
- Sho should look like someone who has not surrendered internally, even while physically confined.
- Agatha-linked softness should be latent, not foregrounded, unless a later variant shows the cloak or a private moment.

## Style Direction

- Grimy swashbuckling fantasy.
- Hard tropical heat, stale air, salt-stained wood, rope, rusted fittings, and punished flesh.
- Pathfinder/Golarion pirate-fantasy tone, not generic medieval prison fantasy.
- Portrait should prioritize recognizable identity and story state over beauty.
