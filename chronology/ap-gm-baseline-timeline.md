## Current Table-Specific Divergence Notes

### Late Wormwood day-label drift

The AP scaffold would make a simple structural count from Wormwood Day 1 through Day 21, but recovered late-Wormwood sources preserve explicit legacy date labels that do not always map cleanly to strict AP counting. Current bridge handling:

- `session-16-to-21-missing-mirei-perspective-summary.md` anchors Day 18 to Erastus 23, Day 19 to Erastus 24, and Day 20/21 to Erastus 25, while retaining 4712 AR as the active year.
- Treat this as recovered table chronology, not an AP error.
- Use AP set-piece order for structure; use recovered dates and GM corrections for campaign chronology.

### Man's Promise boarding date

The boarding date is no longer open:

- Erastus 25, 4712 AR is the locked battle / capture date.
- This is the capture of the Man's Promise, not `Man's Promise Day 1`.
- The Session 5 duplicate label saying Sunday / Erastus 26 is a source-label artifact.
- Erastus 26 is the revel day.
- Erastus 27 is the prisoner / officer execution / skeleton crew / ship split day.

### Man's Promise Day 1 placement

The Man's Promise does not begin its harsher Plugg/Scourge workday until Erastus 28 in the locked campaign timeline. This means:

- Erastus 25 is the capture of the Man's Promise.
- Erastus 26 is the revel day.
- Erastus 27 is transition, transfer, separation, and command setup.
- Erastus 28 is **Man's Promise Day 1**, the first full Plugg-command workday.
- Erastus 29-30 hold the course-change / mutiny-whisper / Mistress Mirei escalation material.

### Storm and Bonewrack placement

Use the AP storm / shipwreck sequence as structural scaffold:

- Man's Promise Day 4 / Erastus 31: storm, hidden grindylow abductions, open attack.
- Man's Promise Day 5 / Arodus 1: ship damage, water shortage, island supply mission.

Use current GM truth for table-specific changes:

- Sandara Quinn and Tilly Brackett are the two initial storm abduction victims.
- Goyle Onórach is captured later during a subsequent grindylow attack while the PCs are away.
- Goyle later reports Plugg knocked him overboard with the Tidal Cutlass while Goyle was fighting a boarder.
