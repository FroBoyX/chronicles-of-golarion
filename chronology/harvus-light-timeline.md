# Harvus Light Timeline

## Document Status

- Type: Character-perspective chronology
- Maturity: Draft
- Source: Harvus light timeline notes
- Canon stance: Relative sequence evidence; absolute dating still requires reconciliation

## Purpose

This document converts Harvus' informal light timeline notes into a usable sequence for Chronicle reconstruction.

Day numbers are preserved as note labels and should not be treated as final calendar truth until reconciled with the master chronology.

## Wake / Day 1 Context

- Harvus wakes aboard the Wormwood.
- He recognizes Jaundiced Jape, Aretta Bansion, and Maheem from the press-ganging context.
- He records first impressions of Sho, Rak, and Mirei.
- He becomes a rigger.
- Sandara Quinn explains Bloody Hour and returns two items.
- Sandara tells the PCs that Grok is superstitious and likes booze and music.
- Harvus identifies Sandara as a healer and is immediately attracted to her.

## First Few Days

- Harvus sees a dwarf around the ship, one of those he associates with the Port Peril attack.
- Sandara tells Harvus she is from Hell Harbor, from a poor family, grew up around the streets, and turned to life at sea.
- Harvus influences Grok to helpful.
- Harvus loots or recovers several items from the bilges.
- Harvus trades a mace for a flail and retrieves it from a wooden locker.

## Day 7 — Heavy Rain

- Harvus gets his ring, necklace, and Rosie's fiddle.

## Day 9

- Harvus makes friends with Rosie Cusswell.

## Day 10

- Rosie is lost.

## Day 11

- Harvus seeks solace with Sandara.
- Harvus swims to a reef, is badly attacked, and is healed by Sandara.

## Day 12

- Harvus influences Barefoot Samms Toppin to friendly.
- Harvus drinks heavily with Sandara and passes out after severe drunkenness.

## Day 13

- Harvus finishes influencing Samms Toppin.

## Day 14

- Riaris Krine runs boarding exercises.
- Harvus performs poorly.
- During later defensive training, Harvus' rage slips.
- Harvus insults Scourge, is lashed, nearly stays conscious, is whipped again, and goes down.

## Day 15

- Harvus works line duty and works diligently.
- He has a conversation with Sandara.
- He influences Ratline.
- He sleeps and heals.

## Day 16

- Harvus notes he is no longer addicted to the drinking contest substance or rum-related aftermath.
- Harvus heavily influences Conchobhar.

## Day 17

- Harvus works in the bilges.
- A spider attempts to bite him; he punches it.
- The work leaves him fatigued.

## Day 18

- Harvus returns to rigging.
- He works diligently and goes to bed early.

## Day 19

- Harvus spends the day in the crow's nest and sees only sea.
- Sho tells Harvus that Scourge tried to have her killed and that she has been boxed.
- Harvus records that the thought of Scourge's villainy makes his blood boil and that he will see Scourge's blood spilled.

## Day 20

- The Wormwood spots a ship.

## Day 21 — Boarding the Man's Promise

- Harvus boards the Man's Promise.
- He is shot repeatedly.
- He kills several people.
- Sho reacts negatively to the amount or style of killing.
- The Wormwood crew takes the ship.
- Festivities begin.
- Harvus spends needed time aiding Sandara's recovery.
- Sho gives Harvus a potion and 275 gold.

## Day 24 — New Boat

- Harvus is aboard the new boat, the Man's Promise.
- He continues rigging work.
- He observes the main hold as charred, looted, and containing large water barrels, stairs, and a trap door by the main mast.
- He socializes with Badger, who seems to like him somewhat.

## Day 25 or 26

- Harvus kisses / smooches Sandara.

## Day 27

- A major storm hits.
- Monsters attack.
- Harvus goes below to help bail water.
- He records hostility toward Slippery Syl.

## Day 28

- Plugg calls roll.
- Sandara is missing.
- Harvus searches the deck with Rak and finds nothing.
- Later, Rak tells the group they must go to land and search for food and supplies.
- Rak gives Harvus Sandara's holy symbol of Besmara.

## Erastus / Arodus 1 — Island Landing

- The party reaches land.
- They find human and monster tracks leading into a marsh.
- They cross a bog.
- Rak is attacked by frog-like creatures.
- The party makes camp.
- On third watch, ghouls attack.

## Day 29

- The group moves through bog and mosquito hazards.
- Harvus is nearly dead after the swamp sequence.
- The party finds the foul tree / rotting camp with lurid paintings, flies, and dead humanoids.
- They burn it.
- They reach a beach and later camp.

## Day 30 — First Riptide Cove Attempt

- Harvus enters the cave and yells for Sandara.
- Stirges attack.
- The party enters the water.
- Harvus identifies seaweed patches with hooks.
- Grindylows attack in numbers; Harvus estimates around eleven.
- Sho goes down.
- One grindylow has Sandara's hat; Harvus marks it for death.

## Day 30 Part 2 — Failed Assault

- Harvus pushes into the cave.
- He is swarmed by grindylows.
- He kills several but takes multiple hits, including a critical.
- He drinks a potion, recovers, and enters a rage.
- He kills the grindylow that crit him by smashing it into a wall.
- The hat-wearing grindylow escapes or is gone.
- Harvus retreats, regroups, falls unconscious, and later wakes overlooking the cliff.
- The group camps as darkness approaches.

## Day 31 — Return to Riptide Cove

- Harvus feels healthier and rage burns hard.
- Harvus returns to the cave with Mirei.
- At the ocean entrance, he jumps from the cliff, crushes one grindylow, spears another, takes readied spear hits, and kills the remaining sentries.
- Harvus and Mirei enter without alerting the lair.

## Day 31 Part 2 — The Cauldron

- Harvus and Mirei move stealthily through tunnels.
- They find a large pool.
- Harvus sees Sandara, the Whale, the grindylow, and the female grindylow.
- He retreats briefly, receives cure light wounds, then returns.
- The captives begin sinking.
- Harvus swims down through the water.
- He sees a devilfish.
- He reaches the bottom and cuts Tilly free, signaling as best he can that he is friendly.
- He gives Tilly his flail.
- He cuts Goyle and Sandara free.
- Sandara seems unresponsive.
- Harvus swims upward with Sandara on his shoulder.
- Tilly appears to be in trouble with an octopus-like creature.
- Harvus harpoons it with Rak's spear, grabs Tilly, and keeps swimming.
- Harvus loses the spear while trying to answer Mirei's message.
- Harvus gets Sandara and Tilly above water.
- He wonders where Goyle is.

## Item Notes

- Harvus recovered his ring and necklace.
- Harvus recovered Rosie's fiddle.
- Harvus received Sandara's holy symbol from Rak before the island search.
- Harvus gave Tilly his flail during the rescue.
- Harvus used Rak's +1 returning spear and likely lost it in the Cauldron pool.

## Relationship Notes

- Grok: helpful.
- Rosie: friend.
- Samms: friendly.
- Ratline: influenced.
- Conchobhar: heavily influenced.
- Badger: warmed somewhat during Man's Promise socializing.
- Sandara: romantic thread developed, including drinking, comfort, recovery care, and kiss.
- Scourge: personal vengeance escalated sharply.
