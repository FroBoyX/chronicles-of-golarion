# Open Threads

This document tracks unresolved campaign threads that may matter for relaunch or future continuity.

## Immediate Threads

### The Fork on Bonewrack Isle

Harvus and Mirei are returning with Sandara, Tilly, and Goyle.

Rak is leading or accompanying Shivikah, Slippery Syl, and Jaundiced Jape toward the same area.

The two groups are about to meet or discover one another.

### Sho in the Hot-Box

Sho is imprisoned aboard the Man's Promise after Scourge's punishment.

She has begun speaking with Kahina Bukekayo, who challenges Sho to prove her remorse by acting against Plugg and Scourge.

### Plugg's Betrayal

The Man's Promise changed course away from Port Peril.

Sandara, Kroop, and others suspect Plugg is taking the ship for himself, likely toward Rickety's Squibs or the Bloodcove region.

### Mirei's Plugg Assignment

Mirei was elevated into Plugg's orbit as "Mistress Mirei" and ordered to prove loyalty through a betrayal mission against the other PCs.

She did not act on the order and has confessed the danger to Harvus and the rescued prisoners.

### Sandara's Rescue

Sandara Quinn survived the grindylow captivity and was rescued by Harvus and Mirei.

Her physical condition, knowledge, and next actions remain immediately relevant.

### Tilly and Goyle

Tilly Brackett and Goyle Onórach were also rescued from the grindylow caverns.

Goyle carries information about Plugg and Scourge's intentions.

### Scourge's Work Party

Master Scourge sent Slippery Syl, Jaundiced Jape, Shivikah, Fipps, and Rak to recover food and water.

Their loyalty, injuries, and response to the rescued group remain volatile.

## Character Threads

### Harvus and Sandara

Harvus has shown intense devotion to Sandara, including returning to the grindylow caves despite danger.

Sandara has shown affection and trust toward Harvus.

### Sho's Morality

Sho continues to resist needless killing and has repeatedly challenged Rak and other pirates over brutality.

Her current captivity creates a chance to act on those convictions.

### Rak's Reputation

Rak has performed genuine rescues and practical leadership, but also used brutal force during the boarding of the Man's Promise.

Different characters view him very differently.

### Mirei's Trust Problem

Mirei has been a quiet background PC with possible traitor elements, but chose not to betray the party when ordered.

Her confession may become a trust hinge.

## Longer-Term Threads

### Rickety's Squibs

Kroop believes Plugg may be taking the Man's Promise to Rickety's Squibs to alter the ship's identity.

### Bloodcove

Bloodcove has been discussed as a dangerous eastern destination and possible reference point for Plugg's course.

### The Motley

The Man's Promise may have had an earlier nameplate reading **the Motley** beneath the current nameplate.

This has not yet been explored in campaign play.

### Harvus' Revenge on Scourge

Harvus knows Scourge was responsible for his gang-pressing and wing injury.

He has prayed to Calistria for revenge.

### Agatha Dreamriver

Sho's relationship with Agatha Dreamriver remains a major backstory thread.

### Magnimar

Sho intends to reach Magnimar someday.

### Rak's Pirate Legacy

Rak is the grandson of Baas'lisk Wraithraiser, an infamous Free Captain.

His legacy remains unresolved.

### Mirei's Destiny

Mirei is an aasimar Sea Singer from the River Kingdoms seeking to understand her destiny.

Her personal discovery remains underdeveloped and should be player-led.
