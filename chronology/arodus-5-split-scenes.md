# Arodus 5 Split-Scene Chronology

## Document Status

- Type: Chronology
- Maturity: Draft, corrected with Tidal Cutlass naming normalization
- Scope: Immediate pre-relaunch split scenes
- Source: GM-provided Arodus 5 chat-log discovery

## Note on Timekeeping

The source log contains out-of-order timestamps and some typographical noise.

This chronology preserves useful time markers but should not be treated as fully reconciled until compared against the full chat logs.

## Morning — After the Riptide Cove Rescue

### Harvus Group

Harvus, Mirei, Sandara, Tilly, and Goyle emerge from the Riptide Cove rescue sequence.

Tilly regains enough awareness to thank Harvus and ask how he found them.

Sandara is unconscious or mostly unresponsive, coughing water and breathing heavily.

Tilly says Sandara took the worst of it and fought hard when the grindylows knocked them off the ship.

Tilly insists they check whether the Man's Promise guard survived because he helped her through the last day.

Goyle surfaces alive, angry, and still pulling a tentacle from his arm.

Harvus helps him ashore.

Goyle warns the group not to expect a warm welcome back at the ship.

Goyle says Plugg blasted him and a grindylow off the ship with the Tidal Cutlass.

Goyle says Plugg is no pirate captain and has no honor.

Goyle also warns that Plugg and Scourge planned to kill the PCs if they returned.

Mirei confesses that she was tasked with killing the PCs to prove loyalty to Plugg's side, but did not.

Harvus carries Sandara and the group makes haste.

## 11:45am — Scourge Questions Rak

Master Scourge questions Rak about food and water.

Rak explains the broken-bridge route.

Scourge revises the plan:

- Slippery Syl leads the supply team.
- Jape, Shivikah, Fipps, and Rak go.
- They are not to stop at the caverns.
- The ship will set sail and meet them on the south beach for coconuts.
- They must bring water from the spring down to the beach.
- They must recover as much food as possible.

## Late Morning / Midday — Broken Bridge and Frogs

Rak leads the group toward the broken bridge.

Syl orders Rak to cross first because he has crossed before.

Rak falls in while attempting to cross.

Frogs attack.

Shivikah dives in to help Rak and stabs a frog.

Fipps is attacked and flails in the water.

Syl uses axe, cutlass, and command pressure to keep the party moving.

Jape struggles in the water but lands a final hit before getting stuck or starting to sink.

The party survives but is injured, embarrassed, and more tense.

## Midday — Botflies and Formation

The party hears or sees signs of the insect swarms Rak had warned about.

Rak wants to move quickly.

Syl stops him and says they move as a group because she needs him alive.

She distributes flasks and establishes formation:

- Jape lead.
- Fipps middle.
- Shivikah guarding Fipps.
- Rak rear.

This proves Syl is not only violent; she is tactical.

## 12:21pm–12:47pm — Sho and Kahina

Sho calls out from captivity aboard the Man's Promise.

Kahina Bukekayo answers.

Kahina tells Sho the ship is setting sail for the far shore to take on supplies, then leaving for an unknown destination.

Kahina confronts Sho over the Man's Promise dead.

Sho expresses remorse and says she wants to live long enough to kill the cretins in charge.

Kahina says words mean nothing and demands a test:

- Would Sho kill those who torment them?
- Would Sho lose her life to end theirs?
- If Sho hesitates, Kahina will ensure Sho dies for her sins.

Sho agrees.

Kahina leaves.

## 12:28pm — Burned Tree

Rak's group reaches the burned tree.

Syl asks what happened.

Rak answers with alchemy-themed humor.

Syl refuses to stop and keeps the group moving.

## 1:30pm — Cornfield Caution

Rak suggests resting before the unexplored cornfields.

Syl refuses to rest before the stronghold.

Fipps is visibly exhausted and sits during the pause.

Syl threatens Rak over insubordination and reminds him she needs him alive, not his opinions.

Rak counters that if she needs him alive, he will move cautiously.

Shivikah looks nervous.

Jape appears disinterested.

The group continues.

## 2:15pm — Coconut Beach

Rak's group passes Coconut Beach.

Rak points out the coconut trees in a tour-guide tone.

Syl says they are going to the stronghold and that the ship should be meeting them at the beach soon.

Syl orders Fipps to stay at the beach.

Everyone else pushes on.

This resolves Fipps' beach location.

## 2:25pm — Moving Beyond Coconut Beach

Rak, Syl, Shivikah, and Jape continue past Coconut Beach toward the stronghold / route ahead.

Fipps remains behind.

## 2:27pm — Last Checkpoint

The survivors stop to rest at their last checkpoint and glare at approaching botflies.

The label `survivors` appears in the source log and likely refers to the moving island-side party, but this should be reconciled.

## 2:28pm — Cold-Open Beat

The path continues.

Rak hears something ahead.

This is the fork collision point.

## Current Scene Alignment

### Harvus Group

Returning with rescued captives and damning information.

### Rak Group

Approaching the fork with Syl, Shivikah, and Jape after leaving Fipps at Coconut Beach.

### Fipps

At Coconut Beach, left there by Syl because the ship is expected to meet there.

### Sho

Still locked up aboard the Man's Promise after agreeing to Kahina's life-for-life test.
