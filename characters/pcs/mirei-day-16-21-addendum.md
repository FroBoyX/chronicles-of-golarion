## Summary

This cache bridges Mirei from post-lashing recovery through the Man's Promise sighting. It gives Mirei's private perspective on influence attempts, social contamination from association with Sho/Harvus, Scourge's absence from performances, Sho in the hot-box, and Mirei joining Sho's aft boarding party.

## New / Sharpened Beats

### Day 16 — Recovery and Influence Attempts

- Mirei remains injured from the mast punishment and recovers slowly.
- She attempts to influence Giffer Tibbs with a 21 and Giffer warms slightly.
- She checks in privately with Sho, saying she talked with Giffer and is still badly hurt.
- She asks Sho whether Sho has made progress making friends.
- Mirei performs with a roll of 22.

### Day 17 — Plugg's Assignment Shift

- Plugg announces he is personally handling assignments, punishment, and task inspections after discipline issues.
- Plugg assigns Mirei to runner duty and says he will take it easy on her.
- This confirms Plugg's increasing direct control before the Man's Promise phase.

### Day 17 — Conchobhar Avoids Mirei

- Mirei tries to speak with Conchobhar after he attended her performance and rolls 25 Diplomacy.
- Conchobhar says he likes her but she hangs around the monk and he does not want association with the PCs.
- He has heard things and tells Mirei to stay away.
- Mirei apologizes and returns to work.
- This is a clear social-contamination beat: Mirei's association with Sho/Harvus/Rak begins costing her crew access.

### Day 17 — Ratline Contact

- Mirei rolls 26 Diplomacy with Ratline after dinner.
- Ratline calls her the bard and says her performances have been improving again.
- Mirei introduces herself properly and asks how long he has been aboard.
- Ratline invites her to sit.
- This adds Ratline as a receptive performance-linked social contact.

### Day 18 — Erastus 23 Legacy Label / Performance 25

- The log explicitly labels Day 18 as Oathday, Erastus 23 in the old 4691 year; archive year should remain 4712.
- Mirei is assigned foredeck repairs.
- Mirei records a performance roll of 25.

### Day 19 — Erastus 24 / Scourge Absence Question

- Plugg assigns Mirei to aft-deck swab duty.
- Mirei asks Plugg where Scourge has been because she has not seen Scourge at her performances for the last few nights.
- Plugg says Scourge is below handling things and that it is not Mirei's business.
- This suggests Mirei was tracking who attended her performances and noticed Scourge's absence.

### Day 19 — Badger Safe Contact

- Mirei rolls 20 to have a friendly conversation with Badger.
- She says she is taking a break from performance because manual work has affected her hands.
- She says she wanted to talk with someone she knows is not trying to see her on the mast.
- This strengthens Badger as one of Mirei's safer Wormwood contacts.

### Day 20 / 21 — Hot-Box Message to Sho

- After the Man's Promise is sighted and Sho is released for the boarding action, Mirei magically messages Sho to ask whether she is all right in the hot-box.
- Sho replies that she is fine, that the hot-box does not affect her the same way it would affect a normal person, and that she may have a way not to die.
- Mirei says she is glad and does not want Sho to die, clarifying that she means ever.
- This is a direct private connection between Mirei and Sho immediately before the boarding.

### Day 20 / 21 — Joining Sho's Aft Boarding Party

- Riaris gives Sho command of an aft boarding party and warns that fog will reduce visibility.
- Sho asks Mirei to join because the situation feels wrong and Sho would rather have Mirei close once the fog comes in.
- Mirei agrees.
- Mirei reports to Grok for a weapon, asks for a shortsword or rapier, and receives a dagger because those are not available.
- Grok also gives Mirei a scroll and tells her to be careful.
- The log ends before the boarding combat begins.
