## Summary

This cache extends Rak's Wormwood social-gambling and rum-pressure arc into Erastus 23-25. It shows Kroop and Grok protecting or managing Rak's rum problem, Tilly and Crimson running a risky con with him, and the social fallout with Maheem, Fipps, and Syl.

## New / Sharpened Beats

### Day 17 — Kroop / Grok Rum Enforcement

- Kroop makes Rak drink with him because Scourge has tied Kroop's safety to Rak's rum compliance.
- Kroop privately tells Grok to make sure Rak drinks because Kroop has been threatened with losing fingers if Rak does not.
- Grok takes over, tells Rak to do it for Ambrose, downs some of the bottle herself, and tells Rak not to make a habit of this.
- This confirms Kroop and Grok both actively protect Rak, but the protection has limits.

### Day 17 — Tilly / Crimson Arm-Wrestling

- Tilly recruits Rak into arm-wrestling against Crimson Cogward.
- Rak wins against Crimson and collects 20 gold.
- Tilly is impressed and proposes a future scheme: she draws people in and Rak knocks them down.
- Rak reveals he hates the rum; Tilly realizes that explains why he altered drinks.

### Day 18 — Tilly / Crimson Con

- Tilly sets up a rematch plan: Rak throws a match to Crimson, then challenges others to prove himself and clean them out.
- Rak warns that bluffing is not his strength.
- Rak throws the match to Crimson, but several observers suspect something.
- Syl threatens Rak when she has money on him and expects him to win.
- Rak then challenges others, including Maheem and Fipps.
- Maheem accuses Rak of cheating and tells him to watch his back.
- Fipps escalates the wager and later says he and others will take the money from Rak's corpse in port.
- Tilly grows nervous and tells Rak to avoid Maheem, Chumlett, and Syl for a while.
- This establishes the gambling con as profitable but socially dangerous.

### Day 19 — Galley / Rum Continuation

- Kroop again pressures Rak to drink at least enough to get rum on his breath.
- Kroop offers to handle one ration himself and check stores, hoping the rum supply may run out.
- Rak says each drink feels like it is killing his insides and that he cannot stand much more.
- This makes Rak's rum problem more than a joke: it is a continuing physical / survival pressure and puts Kroop at risk.

### Day 20 / 21 — Boarding Preparation

- Plugg assigns Rak to cook as the Man's Promise is sighted.
- Harrigan orders pursuit.
- Riaris orders slaughtered pigs dropped over the stern to attract sharks, and Rak is part of carrying out the pig drop.
- Rak says goodbye to Grok before boarding, telling her he will be back and to stay safe.
- Grok supports him with gear/scroll handling in the same preparation sequence.
