## Summary

This cache covers Sho's Erastus 23-25 bridge: continued pressure around rum, the bilges ambush by Aretta and Fipps, Scourge's response, Stitchman's treatment, hot-box confinement, interrupted keelhauling, Riaris' release under captain's orders, and Sho assembling the aft boarding party.

## New / Sharpened Beats

### Day 16 — Sho / Mirei Injury Check

- Sho privately asks Mirei how she is doing.
- Mirei says she talked with Giffer and is still recovering badly.
- Sho asks whether anything might help and then says she may try making friends that night.

### Day 16 — Peppery Warning

- Sho tries to speak with Peppery while Peppery is on duty.
- Peppery warns Sho that she is bad at taking orders and advice.
- Peppery says she has patience, but others do not, and Sho should learn discipline before the whip teaches it.
- Sho accepts the correction and leaves.
- This reinforces Peppery as stern but not sadistic.

### Day 19 — Bilges Ambush

- Plugg assigns Sho to the bilges / rats / pumping.
- Fipps and Aretta confront Sho below and attack her with daggers.
- Sho is badly wounded but survives.
- Sho calls for Sandara to help the two attackers afterward.
- Scourge arrives and frames the fight as crew business, warns Sho about the penalty for killing, and sends her to Stitchman while blocking Sandara's intervention.
- Sho maintains that her back wounds prove she was attacked.
- This is the concrete source for the Aretta death / hot-box trajectory.

### Day 19 — Stitchman Treatment and Disguise Risk

- Habbly Quarne / Stitchman identifies the wounds as dagger wounds.
- He treats Sho's back with a sealing putty or similar shipboard remedy.
- Sho is embarrassed when told to remove the ruined shirt and cloak, but insists the bandages stay.
- This may relate to how physical injury and treatment threatened Sho's presentation / disguise boundaries.

### Day 19 — Kipper Rum Pressure

- At dinner, Kipper sits with Sho and pressures her into a toast despite her wanting to be alone after a traumatic day.
- Harvus arrives and Sho tries to hand her drink to him as a way to avoid drinking.
- Kipper produces enough rum for Harvus too, forcing the social pressure to continue.
- Sho later tells Harvus she cannot drink the stuff and did not want to drag him into it.
- This gives direct evidence of Sho's ongoing rum-avoidance pressure and Harvus helping shield her.

### Day 19 — Plugg Hot-Boxes Sho

- Plugg confronts Sho about the punishment for killing a crew member.
- Sho explains that she asked for Sandara to heal the attackers.
- Plugg says Sandara helped Chumlett, but Aretta is dead.
- Plugg says he lacks time to deal with Sho that night and places her in the hot-box for judgment the next evening.
- Sho enters the box and uses meditative breathing.

### Day 20 / 21 — Interrupted Keelhauling

- Plugg announces a crew death and says Sho will be keelhauled at Bloody Hour for killing Aretta while defending herself.
- Sho shouts from the box that two people dead makes less sense.
- Scourge kicks the box and tells her to think about chain of command.
- The Man's Promise is sighted before Bloody Hour.
- Riaris releases Sho from the hot-box under captain's orders.
- Riaris says the charges may be dropped if Sho proves herself by leading an aft boarding party and completing the assigned tasks.
- This confirms Sho's command role as a coercive survival bargain, not a voluntary leadership bid.

### Day 20 / 21 — Aft Party Assembly

- Riaris orders Sho to take the wheel, kill guards by the sterncastle, guard the ship's boats, and hold position despite fog.
- Sho recruits Harvus immediately.
- Crimson refuses to join and assumes Harvus may have helped release Sho.
- Giffer tells Sho that if she has officer orders, she should stop asking and start ordering.
- Owlbear is unavailable because he will be with Plugg.
- Sandara is unavailable because she is assigned to Scourge's boarding party.
- Sho asks Mirei to join because fog makes the situation feel wrong and she wants Mirei close.
- The log ends before the boarding combat.
