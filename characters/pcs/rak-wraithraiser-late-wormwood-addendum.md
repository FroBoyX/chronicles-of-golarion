## Addendum Beats For Main Rak Dossier

### Erastus 23 — Tilly Con and Rising Hostility

- Rak and Tilly continued their gambling / arm-wrestling partnership.
- The con against Crimson and others worked only partly.
- Rak's poor bluffing or conduct created hostility with Maheem, Fipps, and Syl.
- Tilly became nervous and warned Rak to avoid Maheem, Chumlett, and Syl for a while.

**Merge note:** This is the clean pre-Man's Promise placement for Rak's tension with Fipps and Syl.

### Erastus 25 — Man's Promise Boarding

- Rak joined Sho's aft boarding party during the Man's Promise capture.
- Rak killed at least one pleading or downed Rahadoumi sailor.
- Sho objected and tried to impose restraint.
- Rak's harsher violence and Sho's attempts to control it became a major moral split.
- Rak was involved in a lifeboat incident on the starboard side; exact casualty result remains unresolved.
- A private whisper preserves Rak's impulse to get rid of Giffer / toss her over.

**GM-only note:** GM truth currently has Giffer unconscious but stable, then Rak intentionally stomped/trampled her while moving through the fog, destabilizing her and causing death. This must remain GM-side unless magic, confession, or equivalent proof exposes it.

**Player-facing note:** Do not tell Rak's player as a revealed archive fact unless the GM intends that knowledge to become explicit. Rak-facing material can preserve his known conduct and consequences without exposing hidden adjudication text.

### Erastus 26 — Harvus Suspicion Exchange

- During the post-battle revel aftermath, Harvus privately asked Rak whether Rak had anything to do with Giffer no longer being with them.
- Rak said he saw Giffer lying on the ground while moving toward another boat to stop escapees, but was not sure whether she was alive or dead.
- Harvus accepted Rak's answer at the time.

**Merge note:** This is a suspicion boundary, not proof. Keep it separate from GM truth.

### Erastus 27 — Grok Separation and Transfer

- During the ship split, Kroop told Rak to help pack because they were heading with the new ship.
- Grok did not come with Rak.
- Grok held back tears and gave Rak a present before he left.
- Rak's separation from Grok is both emotional and item-relevant.

**Merge note:** Cross-reference Rak/Grok relationship and current item questions.

### Erastus 28 — Borrowed Fiddle to Mirei

- On the first Plugg-command workday, Mirei lacked her harp and needed an instrument for Plugg's ordered performance.
- Rak lent Mirei the fiddle he had received from Grok.
- Mirei used the borrowed fiddle for the first Plugg performance.

**Merge note:** Rak knows he helped Mirei perform; he does not necessarily know the full private coercion inside Plugg's cabin.

### Erastus 29-30 — Course Change / Rickety's Squibs Channel

- Kroop called Rak for a private talk in the galley and included Sandara.
- Kroop said Plugg should not be expected to follow orders now that Harrigan was out of sight.
- Sandara confirmed the ship was going east.
- Kroop identified Rickety's Squibs as the likely destination and explained squibbing a ship.
- Sandara told Rak and Kroop to keep the information between them and trust no one.

**Merge note:** This places Rak inside an early anti-Plugg information channel before Bonewrack makes Plugg's betrayal more obvious.

### Erastus 31 / Arodus 1 — Storm and First Island Supply Mission

- During storm / grindylow chaos, Rak took heavy damage and needed healing.
- Sho checked whether Rak was all right.
- Sho later reported that the grindylows seemed unusually interested in Rak.
- Plugg sent Rak by rowboat with Sho, Mirei, and Harvus to gather fresh water and supplies.
- Plugg told Kroop / Ambrose that the water barrels were ruptured and the ship needed supplies.

**Merge note:** Do not assume Rak knows Mirei's hidden Plugg task or private alchemist-fire placement.

### Arodus 5 — Syl Supply Party Branch

- Scourge questioned Rak about food, water, and routes over the swamp.
- Rak reported the broken-bridge route.
- Scourge changed the supply plan: Syl would lead the recovery team, the ship would sail and meet them at Coconut Beach, and the team would not stop at the caverns.
- Scourge ordered Syl to take Jape, Shivikah, Fipps, and Rak.
- Rak led the group to the broken bridge and fell into the water during the crossing.
- Frogs attacked Rak and Fipps.
- Shivikah helped Rak by cutting the frog's tongue loose.
- Syl coordinated enough to keep Rak alive because she needed him.
- Fipps panicked; Jape eventually landed the final hit.
- The party reached the burned tree around 12:28 PM.
- Around 1:30 PM, Rak argued for caution before the cornfields and resisted Syl's demand to push recklessly.
- The party passed Coconut Beach; Syl ordered Fipps to stay there because the ship should meet them.
- Rak, Syl, Shivikah, and Jape pushed inland.
- Current endpoint: Rak hears something ahead at Arodus 5, 2:28 PM.

**Merge note:** This is Rak's current cold-open branch. Do not import Riptide rescue confession or Sho/Kahina bargain into Rak's knowledge.
