## Summary

This cache bridges Harvus from the aftermath of the mass mast punishment through Sho's release and the Man's Promise boarding setup. It emphasizes Sandara's need for neutrality, Harvus' concern for Sho after the bilges ambush, and his immediate willingness to join Sho's aft boarding party.

## New / Sharpened Beats

### Day 17 — Sandara Warns About Polarization

- Harvus asks Sandara whether she has heard anything about Plugg pulling him from rigging and assigning him to the bilges.
- Sandara warns that people are avoiding association with Harvus and Sho because of the attention they drew.
- She says things are getting polarized on the Wormwood.
- Sandara explains she is trying to remain neutral so she can watch everyone's backs without being caught up in the factional pressure.
- Harvus offers distance because he does not want harm to come to her, but Sandara says that is not what she meant and tells him she has his back.
- This builds on the Day 15 rage counsel: Sandara is not withdrawing affection, but she is preserving operational neutrality.

### Day 19 — Harvus Helps Sho Avoid Rum Pressure

- Kipper pressures Sho to toast despite her trying to be alone after the bilges ambush.
- Harvus arrives, and Sho tries to hand him her rum as a way to avoid drinking it.
- Kipper supplies rum for Harvus too, forcing the social pressure to continue.
- Sho later privately tells Harvus she cannot drink the stuff and did not want to drag him into the situation.
- Harvus checks on Sho and learns Fipps and Aretta attacked her in the bilges.
- Harvus reacts with anger toward Scourge's involvement and says he would have had Sho's back.
- Sho says everyone tells her to bide her time, but it is hard when enemies seek her out to remove her from the picture.
- Harvus notices she is trembling and tells her she need not put on a show for him.

### Day 19 — Sho Hot-Boxed

- Plugg places Sho in the hot-box after Aretta's death.
- Harvus internally recognizes the danger but cannot intervene directly.
- Sandara later sees Harvus coming and tells him to go to bed, indicating she is working and cannot be seen pulled into the situation.

### Day 20 / 21 — Sho Released and Harvus Joins

- Riaris releases Sho from the hot-box under captain's orders and assigns her an aft boarding party.
- Sho immediately asks Harvus to join, and Harvus agrees.
- Harvus and Sho attempt to find more people for the team.
- Harvus tries to recruit Sandara, but Sandara is assigned to Scourge's boarding party under Plugg's order and cannot join.
- Sandara questions why Harvus is assisting rather than Sho recruiting directly, which reinforces the oddness of Sho being placed in command under duress.
- Harvus returns to the team before the boarding begins.
