# Tilly Brackett

## Document Status

- Type: NPC dossier
- Maturity: Draft
- Scope: Wormwood sailor, rescued captive, witness to Riptide Cove

## Overview

Tilly Brackett is a tough, rum-loving Wormwood sailor who was captured by grindylows and rescued from Riptide Cove.

Her survival matters because she is a familiar Wormwood-side crew member who can witness what happened in the lair and who survived it.

She is not merely a rescue marker. She is one of the people whose continued presence can shape the crew's understanding of Harvus, Mirei, Sandara, and Plugg.

## Identity

- Name: Tilly Brackett
- Type: NPC
- Original role: Wormwood swab
- Baseline personality: Tough joker, likes rum
- Current location: Bonewrack Isle, returning from Riptide Cove
- Current status: Alive, rescued

## Baseline Adventure Path Role

Tilly begins as one of the lower Wormwood crew members who can become ally, neutral party, or future mutiny participant depending on PC interaction.

She is not a major officer but matters because the lower crew's loyalties shape the eventual conflict with Plugg and Scourge.

## Campaign Role

Tilly became one of the missing captives on Bonewrack Isle.

Her apparent loss or capture helped make the island personal rather than only logistical.

Her survival gives the returning Harvus group another witness and another life saved.

## Capture

Tilly was taken by grindylows during the storm or associated chaos around Bonewrack Isle.

She was held in Riptide Cove with Sandara and later Goyle.

She was wounded, exhausted, and in immediate danger during the rescue.

## Rescue

Tilly was rescued by Harvus and Mirei during the same event that saved Sandara and Goyle.

Harvus prioritized Sandara emotionally, but Tilly's survival is still part of the rescue's full consequence.

After escape, Tilly asked about Goyle and showed concern for whether he survived.

## Relationships

## Campaign Function at Relaunch

Tilly's presence can humanize the rescued group.

She is less politically explosive than Goyle, but potentially more relatable to the lower Wormwood crew.

She can confirm:

- the grindylow lair was real,
- Sandara was alive,
- Harvus and Mirei returned for the captives,
- and Plugg's claim that missing sailors were simply gone is incomplete or false.

## Possible Stances After Return

Tilly may support:

- Harvus personally,
- Sandara's judgment,
- any immediate action that prevents Plugg and Scourge from killing people,
- or a practical survival-first approach.

Her exact political stance should emerge from play and prior influence history.
