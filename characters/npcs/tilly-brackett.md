# Tilly Brackett

## Document Status

- Type: NPC dossier
- Maturity: Draft, expanded with Arodus 5 split-scene log
- Scope: Wormwood sailor, rescued captive, witness to Riptide Cove

## Overview

Tilly Brackett is a tough, rum-loving Wormwood sailor who was captured by grindylows and rescued from Riptide Cove.

Her survival matters because she is a familiar Wormwood-side crew member who can witness what happened in the lair and who survived it.

She is not merely a rescue marker. She is one of the people whose continued presence can shape the crew's understanding of Harvus, Mirei, Sandara, Goyle, and Plugg.

## Identity

- Name: Tilly Brackett
- Type: NPC
- Original role: Wormwood swab
- Baseline personality: Tough joker, likes rum
- Current location: Bonewrack Isle, returning from Riptide Cove
- Current status: Alive, rescued
- Current condition: Weak but ambulatory; can walk, but falters

## Baseline Adventure Path Role

Tilly begins as one of the lower Wormwood crew members who can become ally, neutral party, or future mutiny participant depending on PC interaction.

She is not a major officer but matters because the lower crew's loyalties shape the eventual conflict with Plugg and Scourge.

## Campaign Role

Tilly became one of the missing captives on Bonewrack Isle.

Her apparent loss or capture helped make the island personal rather than only logistical.

Her survival gives the returning Harvus group another witness and another life saved.

## Capture

Tilly was taken by grindylows during the storm or associated chaos around Bonewrack Isle.

She was held in Riptide Cove with Sandara and later Goyle.

She was wounded, exhausted, and in immediate danger during the rescue.

Tilly reports that the captives were stripped down to clothing and believes they were intended as dinner.

## Rescue

Tilly was rescued by Harvus and Mirei during the same event that saved Sandara and Goyle.

Harvus prioritized Sandara emotionally, but Tilly's survival is still part of the rescue's full consequence.

After escape, Tilly coughed and thanked Harvus, saying she did not think they were going to make it.

When Harvus asked whether she could walk, she said she thought she would be alright and joked weakly that she had swum.

She knelt beside Sandara and said Sandara took the worst of it.

She also said Sandara gave the grindylows hell when they knocked the captives off the ship.

## Concern for Goyle

Tilly immediately asked about the Man's Promise guard who had been held with them.

She said he had helped her through the last day and insisted they check whether he made it.

When Goyle surfaced alive, Tilly was visibly relieved, limped to him, glomped him, and sank against his chest.

This confirms a meaningful emotional bond or at least strong survival attachment between Tilly and Goyle.

## Relationships

### Harvus G'ron

Harvus helped save Tilly from Riptide Cove.

Tilly is a direct witness to Harvus' heroics and to the risks he took.

Harvus also physically caught her when she faltered after insisting they look for Goyle.

### Sandara Quinn

Tilly was captured and rescued alongside Sandara.

She says Sandara took the worst of the abuse and fought hard when captured.

Tilly likely understands Sandara's condition and the danger of the lair from direct experience.

### Goyle Onórach

Tilly showed immediate concern for Goyle after the rescue.

She says he helped her through the last day.

Her relief on seeing him alive was physical and emotional.

This relationship should be treated as meaningful even if its exact nature remains open.

### Mirei

Mirei helped make the rescue possible.

Tilly was present when Mirei confessed that Plugg's side tasked her with killing the PCs to prove loyalty, and Tilly reacted with shock.

Tilly may be an important lower-crew witness to Mirei choosing rescue instead of betrayal.

### Lower Wormwood Crew

Tilly can affect the opinions of other neutral or friendly lower-crew sailors if she returns alive.

## Current State

- Location: Bonewrack Isle, with Harvus group
- Status: Alive, rescued
- Condition: Weak, shaken, ambulatory but faltering
- Immediate leverage: Witness to the rescue, Sandara's condition, Goyle's survival, and Mirei's confession

## Campaign Function at Relaunch

Tilly's presence can humanize the rescued group.

She is less politically explosive than Goyle, but potentially more relatable to the lower Wormwood crew.

She can confirm:

- the grindylow lair was real,
- Sandara was alive,
- Sandara fought hard when captured,
- Harvus and Mirei returned for the captives,
- Goyle helped her survive captivity,
- and Plugg's claim that missing sailors were simply gone is incomplete or false.

## Possible Stances After Return

Tilly may support:

- Harvus personally,
- Sandara's judgment,
- Goyle's warning,
- any immediate action that prevents Plugg and Scourge from killing people,
- or a practical survival-first approach.

Her exact political stance should emerge from play and prior influence history.
