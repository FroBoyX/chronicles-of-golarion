## Overview

"Caulky" Tarroon is a Harrigan-side shipboard figure recovered in the Erastus 16 reefclaw dinner scene.

She is close enough to the captain's table to fetch dinner, introduce Rak as the source of the meal, and manage the food presentation.

## Erastus 16 Reefclaw Dinner

After Rak caught the big reefclaw / source of dinner, Caulky came to the galley and asked who had caught it.

Kroop confirmed it was Rak.

Caulky was not pleased:

> "It had to be you huh?"

She told Rak the captain wanted to see him and warned him to keep his perkiness down.

Caulky then brought Rak and the dinner to Harrigan, curtsying as she introduced Rak as the source of dinner.

During the meal, she cut and tasted the food, then nearly choked at Rak's description of the fight.

She called him a jakenape and excused herself.
