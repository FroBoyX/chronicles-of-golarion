# Barefoot Samms Toppin

## Document Status

- Type: NPC dossier
- Maturity: Draft
- Scope: Lower-crew NPC, possible Man's Promise ally or witness

## Overview

Barefoot Samms Toppin is a former fisherwoman from the Sodden Lands who never wears shoes.

She is part of the lower-crew social fabric that determines whether the end of Book One becomes a clean mutiny, a fractured standoff, or an unstable survival coalition.

Her exact current placement should be confirmed, but she is likely relevant to the Man's Promise crew state.

## Identity

- Name: Barefoot Samms Toppin
- Type: NPC
- Background: Former fisherwoman from the Sodden Lands
- Baseline role: Wormwood rigger
- Baseline alignment: Chaotic Good
- Current location: Likely Man's Promise, pending confirmation
- Current status: Presumed alive

## Baseline Adventure Path Role

Samms begins as an indifferent lower-crew NPC who can be influenced by the PCs.

She is not part of the initial hostile bloc.

As a rigger and sailor, she also represents practical ship competence.

## Campaign Role

Samms is important because she may be one of the lower-crew sailors capable of moving from neutrality to support.

She has observed the PCs under pressure and may have had direct conversations with Harvus about the Man's Promise course change.

## Campaign History

### Wormwood

Samms served as a rigger aboard the Wormwood.

### Man's Promise

Recovered context suggests Samms transferred to the Man's Promise and spoke with Harvus about the course change away from Port Peril.

She indicated concern and likely recognized that Plugg's course was dangerous.

### Bonewrack / Scourge Shore Pressure

There is recovered context suggesting Samms may have been present during the confrontation involving Rak and Sho, and that she may have felt bad about being forced into violence or pressure.

This needs confirmation.

## Relationships

### Harvus G'ron

Samms may have trusted Harvus enough to discuss the course change or warn him.

This could matter if Harvus returns with rescued witnesses.

### Sandara Quinn

Samms may align with or trust Sandara if both are aboard the Man's Promise and aware of Plugg's betrayal.

### Plugg and Scourge

Samms is not naturally part of the hostile bloc, but she may be constrained by fear, discipline, or immediate orders.

### Rak and Sho

If Samms was present when Scourge's group confronted Rak and Sho, her reaction may affect whether she can later be recruited or trusted.

## Current State

- Location: Likely Man's Promise, pending confirmation
- Status: Presumed alive
- Political state: Potentially movable, likely survival-minded and possibly anti-Plugg if given proof and a viable alternative

## Campaign Function at Relaunch

Samms can represent the non-hostile lower crew who are not automatically heroic.

She may care most about:

- whether the ship can sail,
- whether Plugg will kill people,
- whether the PCs are competent,
- and whether supporting them is survivable.
