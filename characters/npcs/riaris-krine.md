## Overview

Riaris Krine is a Wormwood officer associated with Harrigan's martial culture and practical pirate competence.

In the Erastus 16 reefclaw dinner scene, she is less concerned with Rak's manner than with the fact that he brought good food.

## Identity

## Erastus 16 Reefclaw Dinner

When Caulky brought Rak before Harrigan as the source of dinner, Riaris was present for the meal.

She wanted the food brought on and asked whether the reefclaw had been feisty.

Rak described the fight in his own strange style.

Riaris accepted the practical result:

> "Good enough for me! Dig in."

After Peppery called Rak strange, Riaris again reduced the matter to competence:

> "Caught good food, good enough for me."

## Campaign Use

Riaris can be used as a contrast to Scourge and Plugg. She may still be harsh or dangerous, but this scene shows a practical officer's eye: useful performance counts.
