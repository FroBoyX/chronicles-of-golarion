# Jaundiced Jape

## Document Status

- Type: NPC dossier
- Maturity: Draft
- Scope: Hostile lower-crew NPC, current Bonewrack fork participant

## Overview

Jaundiced Jape is a humorless, greedy half-orc mute and one of the hostile lower-crew pirates associated with Plugg and Scourge's side of the shipboard conflict.

At the relaunch point, he is with Rak Wraithraiser, Shivikah, and Slippery Syl Lonegan on Bonewrack Isle.

Jape matters because he is present at the fork and can turn uncertainty into violence without needing to dominate the conversation.

## Identity

- Name: Jaundiced Jape
- Type: NPC
- Ancestry: Half-orc
- Baseline role: Wormwood swab
- Baseline alignment: Chaotic Evil
- Current location: Bonewrack Isle, with Rak's moving group
- Current status: Alive
- Communication: Mute

## Baseline Adventure Path Role

Jape is one of the hostile lower-crew NPCs who begins aligned against the PCs and closer to Plugg and Scourge's faction.

His presence contributes to the final mutiny's possible enemy numbers.

## Campaign Role

Jape is a low-dialogue pressure character.

Because he is mute, he does not need to argue to be dangerous.

He can communicate through posture, knives, violence, and where he chooses to stand.

## Campaign History

### Wormwood

Jape was part of the hostile lower crew aboard the Wormwood.

He participated in or aligned with Scourge's early harassment structure against the PCs.

### Man's Promise

Jape transferred to the Man's Promise prize crew and remained in the hostile bloc.

### Bonewrack Supply Party

Jape is currently part of the island supply party moving with Rak, Shivikah, and Syl.

He has survived the island hazards to this point.

Recovered context suggests he was involved in the frog/mire sequence and may have struggled in the water before recovering or landing a final blow.

## Relationships

### Rak Wraithraiser

Rak is currently useful as a guide.

Jape likely does not trust him.

Rak's physicality and pirate confidence may matter more to Jape than speeches.

### Slippery Syl Lonegan

Syl may be the more dominant hostile personality in the group.

Jape's response to her leadership or opportunism should be reconstructed through play.

### Shivikah

Shivikah and Jape are both hostile or unfriendly lower-crew presences, but Shivikah may be more situationally flexible.

### Plugg and Scourge

Jape is part of the hostile bloc that benefits from Plugg and Scourge's authority.

Whether this is loyalty, fear, greed, or convenience remains open.

## Current State

- Location: Bonewrack Isle, near the fork route
- Companions: Rak, Shivikah, Slippery Syl
- Not with group: Fipps, at the beach
- Objective: Recover food and water, or at least return useful enough to survive Plugg's command
- Immediate risk: collision with rescued Harvus group

## Campaign Function at Relaunch

Jape is a silent threat.

At the fork, he can make the encounter unstable because he may:

- attack without warning,
- follow Syl's lead,
- back the stronger side,
- try to silence Goyle,
- or surrender if the hostile faction collapses.

## What Not To Assume

Do not assume Jape is stupid.

Do not assume he needs dialogue to be present in a scene.

Do not assume his hostility is ideological; it may be practical, greedy, or survival-driven.
