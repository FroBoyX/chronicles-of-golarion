## Overview

Conchobhar Turlach Shortstone is a handsome, honey-tongued gnome bard whose appearance suggests someone pretending to be a pirate rather than a hardened sailor.

He was one of the five press-ganged new hands in the Erastus 5 Wormwood crew intake, took part in the early Wormwood trials, became a rigger, and quickly faded into the background as an NPC after the first few days.

Harvus' light timeline records that Harvus strongly influenced Conchobhar during the Wormwood period.

## Appearance and Bearing

Conchobhar wears a foppish purple hat, an eye patch, a white silk shirt, and carries a dandyish cane.

His self-presentation is theatrical, self-involved, and dandyish.

His appearance is that of someone pretending to be a pirate, rather than a true buccaneer.

## Baseline Adventure Path Role

Conchobhar is a professional gambler who made the mistake of drinking with the Wormwood's crew one night and failed to notice the cosh in a crew member's hand until it was too late.

He had been aboard less than 24 hours at the start of the adventure.

Conchobhar is resigned to his fate but determined to make the most of it, as he has always yearned for the sea anyway.

One thing dominates Conchobhar's thinking: himself.

Conchobhar is also a serial womanizer.

## Combat / Morale

Conchobhar prefers to talk rather than fight.

If he must fight, he lurks at the edge of combat, preferring to flank or attack with his bow.

Conchobhar flees if reduced to 7 hit points or fewer, but he fights to the death for any female character he is on friendly or better terms with.

Female characters gain a +2 bonus on all checks to influence him.

## Campaign Role

Conchobhar is part of the early press-ganged social cluster, but he is not an automatic ally.

He helps show that not everyone forced aboard the Wormwood becomes a straightforward member of the same cause.

He can be charming, cowardly, useful, irritating, and unexpectedly loyal depending on relationship state.

Harvus' influence may make Conchobhar more available as a later social actor than his baseline selfishness would suggest.

## Campaign History

### Press-Ganged

Conchobhar was taken after drinking with Wormwood crew and failing to notice the cosh before it was too late.

He had been aboard less than a day when the campaign began.

He was one of the five press-ganged new hands in the Erastus 5 crew intake alongside Mirei, Harvus, Rak, and Sho.

### Wormwood Trials

Conchobhar took part in the early Wormwood trials.

He became a rigger under Plugg's authority.

After the first few days, he quickly faded into the background as an NPC.

### Wormwood

He served as a rigger and interacted with the crew through gambling, flirtation, performance, and self-interest.

He found Rosie Cusswell magnificent.

Harvus' light notes record that he "influenced the hell out of Conchobhar" during the Wormwood sequence.
