# Conchobhar Turlach Shortstone

## Document Status

- Type: NPC dossier
- Maturity: Draft
- Scope: Press-ganged gnome bard, lower-crew social actor, Man's Promise placement unresolved

## Overview

Conchobhar Turlach Shortstone is a handsome, honey-tongued gnome bard whose appearance suggests someone pretending to be a pirate rather than a hardened sailor.

He was press-ganged shortly before the PCs and serves as a social mirror: selfish, charming, frightened, opportunistic, and capable of bravery under very specific circumstances.

## Identity

- Name: Conchobhar Turlach Shortstone
- Type: NPC
- Ancestry: Gnome
- Baseline class / role: Bard, rigger
- Current location: Likely Man's Promise, pending confirmation
- Current status: Presumed alive

## Appearance and Bearing

Conchobhar wears a foppish purple hat, an eye patch, a white silk shirt, and carries a dandyish cane.

His self-presentation is theatrical and self-involved.

He looks like someone playing at piracy, but he is not useless.

## Baseline Adventure Path Role

Conchobhar is one of the recent recruits aboard the Wormwood, like the PCs.

He begins unfriendly but can be influenced, especially by female characters.

He is self-centered and prefers talk to fighting.

If reduced in combat, he tends to flee unless defending a friendly-or-better female character.

## Campaign Role

Conchobhar is part of the early press-ganged social cluster.

He helps show that not everyone forced aboard the Wormwood becomes a straightforward ally.

He can be charming, cowardly, useful, irritating, and unexpectedly loyal depending on relationship state.

## Campaign History

### Press-Ganged

Conchobhar was taken after drinking with Wormwood crew and failing to notice the cosh before it was too late.

He had been aboard less than a day when the campaign began.

### Wormwood

He served as a rigger and interacted with the crew through gambling, flirtation, performance, and self-interest.

He found Rosie Cusswell magnificent, despite Rosie's irritation with him.

### Man's Promise

Conchobhar likely transferred to the Man's Promise prize crew under Plugg, but exact current placement should be confirmed.

## Relationships

### Rosie Cusswell

Conchobhar admired Rosie.

Rosie loathed his attention but secretly liked his style in the published baseline.

Rosie's death at sea may affect him if that relationship developed at all in campaign.

### Mirei

Conchobhar and Mirei share bardic/performance space, but their relationship needs reconstruction.

Mirei's position under Plugg may have affected Conchobhar's view of her.

### PCs Generally

Conchobhar is not automatically a party ally.

He may support whoever protects his interests, status, and survival.

### Plugg and Scourge

Conchobhar is unlikely to admire Plugg or Scourge, but fear and self-preservation may constrain him.

## Campaign Function at Relaunch

Conchobhar can complicate the idea that all press-ganged people naturally unite.

He may resent Plugg and Scourge while still hesitating to support a dangerous mutiny.

He may also value the social status of whoever emerges as captain.
