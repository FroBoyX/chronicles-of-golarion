## Summary

This cache strengthens the final Wormwood approach to the Man's Promise. It is especially useful for Plugg, Scourge, Riaris, Sandara, Grok, Kroop, Tilly, Crimson, Fipps, Aretta, Badger, Conchobhar, Ratline, Kipper, Stitchman, Giffer, Owlbear, and Caulky.

## NPC Updates

### Mr. Plugg

- Takes over crew assignments and punishment after discipline issues.
- Says he will personally check task progress.
- Announces the Wormwood has left the Shackles and is sailing south toward merchants and plunder.
- Assigns people in ways that look protective but are clearly controlling: Harvus to bilges for the wing, Sho to rats, Mirei to runner / swab tasks.
- Hot-boxes Sho after Aretta dies from the bilges ambush and schedules keelhauling for Bloody Hour.
- Uses Sho's case as public spectacle until the Man's Promise sighting interrupts.

### Master Scourge

- Is absent from Mirei's performances for several nights because he is below handling matters.
- Appears after the bilges ambush and treats it as crew business rather than an attempted killing.
- Threatens Sho over insubordination and blocks Sandara from simply intervening.
- Threatens Sandara directly and references unfinished business once in port.
- Kicks Sho's hot-box after Plugg announces the keelhauling and frames the situation as chain of command.

### Riaris Krine

- Releases Sho from the hot-box under captain's orders after the Man's Promise is sighted.
- Gives Sho a coercive chance to prove herself: if Sho leads an aft boarding party successfully, the charges can be dropped.
- Orders Sho to take the wheel, kill guards near the sterncastle, guard the ship's boats, and hold position through fog.
- Threatens Sho if she fails, but also gives her real command authority within the assigned mission.
- Orders pigs thrown overboard to attract sharks before the boarding action.

### Sandara Quinn

- Warns Harvus that people are avoiding association with him and Sho because things are polarizing aboard the Wormwood.
- Says she is trying to remain neutral so she can still watch the PCs' backs.
- Is threatened by Scourge after Sho asks for help in the bilges aftermath.
- Is assigned to Scourge's boarding party under Plugg's order and cannot join Sho/Harvus.

### Cut-Throat Grok

- Helps manage Rak's rum problem when Kroop asks her to ensure he drinks enough to protect Kroop from Scourge's threat.
- Gives Mirei a dagger before the Man's Promise boarding when no shortsword or rapier is available.
- Gives Mirei a scroll and tells her to be careful.
- Receives Rak's pre-boarding goodbye and practical affection.

### Ambrose "Fishguts" Kroop

- Continues protecting Rak around the rum requirement by drinking some of it, trying to get Rak to drink enough to smell of rum, and checking the stores.
- Warns Rak that refusal could cost Kroop his fingers or life.
- Uses cooking and toasts as cover for managing the rum issue.

### Tilly Brackett

- Partners with Rak in arm-wrestling/gambling.
- Recognizes Rak's strength and proposes a setup where she draws marks in and Rak beats them.
- Later becomes nervous when Rak's poor bluffing angers Maheem, Fipps, and Syl.
- Warns Rak to avoid them for a while.

### Crimson Cogward

- Arm-wrestles Rak, loses once, then participates in a fixed rematch where Rak throws the contest.
- Later avoids Sho and Harvus when Sho tries to recruit him for the aft boarding party, not wanting to be implicated.

### Fipps Chumlett and Aretta Bansion

- Ambush Sho in the bilges with daggers after accusing her of slacking.
- Sho survives, but Aretta dies after the incident.
- Fipps survives after Sandara's help, according to Plugg's later statement.
- Their attack directly causes Sho's hot-boxing and scheduled keelhauling.

### Habbly Quarne / Stitchman

- Treats Sho's dagger wounds after Scourge sends her to him.
- Identifies the wounds as dagger wounds and uses a practical sealing treatment.
- Requires access to Sho's wounded back but does not need more than the back exposed.

### Kipper

- Pressures Sho to toast despite her trying to be alone after the bilges ambush.
- Provides rum for Harvus too when Sho tries to route her drink to him.
- Frames the toast as eye-to-eye social pressure.

### Badger Medlar

- Serves as a safer contact for Mirei after repeated mast trauma.
- Mirei approaches Badger because Badger is someone she knows is not trying to see her on the mast.

### Conchobhar Shortstone

- Tells Mirei he likes her but does not want association with the PCs because she hangs around the monk and he has heard ugly rumors.
- This is strong evidence of social contamination around PC association.

### Ratline Rattsberger

- Greets Mirei as the bard and says her performances are improving again.
- Invites her to sit and talk after she introduces herself properly.
- Reinforces Ratline as receptive to performance and not automatically hostile.

### Giffer Tibbs

- Warms slightly to Mirei after a successful influence attempt.
- Later tells Sho that if Riaris gave her orders to form a party, Sho should stop asking and start ordering.
- Joins or reports for Sho's aft boarding party if Sho's orders are legitimate.

### Owlbear Hartshorn

- Continues talking with Sho and accepts food.
- Says he can break his chains if needed but sees no need; he gets food and bides his time.
- Cannot join Sho's aft boarding party because he will be with Plugg.

### Caulky Tarroon

- Continues appearing in the galley for officers' food and remains irritated by Rak's presence.
- Kroop uses her arrival as cover to manage Rak's rum situation.
