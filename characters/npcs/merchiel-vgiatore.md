## Campaign Role

Merchiel helps fill out the named Man's Promise survivor bloc and may matter practically because the Man's Promise needs riggers if it is to remain usable after Plugg falls.

Merchiel should not be treated as automatically friendly to Sho or the PCs merely because he is not part of the Wormwood hostile bloc.

The first-night scene suggests Merchiel may be more practically restrained than Nor'gal in the immediate aftermath of the capture. He is angry survivor bloc, not automatic ally, but he can see when a fight is not worth it.

## First Man's Promise Night

Early in the departure of the Man's Promise, Rak tried to talk to Nor'gal and Merchiel.

Nor'gal threatened Rak while visibly shaking with anger.

Merchiel held Nor'gal back and said:

> "It's not worth it..."

This is currently Merchiel's first active recovered characterization. He restrains Nor'gal rather than escalating, suggesting a practical survival instinct.
