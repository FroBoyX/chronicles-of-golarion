## Overview

Peppery Longfarthing is a Wormwood officer presence associated with Harrigan's command layer.

The recovered Erastus 16 reefclaw dinner scene preserves only a small beat for her, but Sho's player answers establish Peppery as a significant figure in Sho's understanding of authority.

Sho reads Peppery as cold, dangerous, competent, attractive, and not obviously malicious.

## Erastus 16 Reefclaw Dinner

After Rak was brought before Harrigan as the source of the reefclaw dinner and rewarded with the return of his confiscated equipment, Peppery observed:

> "What a strange creature."

Riaris Krine answered the practical side of it, saying he had caught good food and that was good enough for her.

## Sho's View of Peppery

Sho is attracted to Peppery for several overlapping reasons:

- confidence based on competence,
- command presence,
- the sense that Peppery can act on anything she sets in motion,
- mystery,
- beauty,
- and Sho's tendency to be drawn toward confident, capable women.

Sho is embarrassed by this attraction and would likely never bring it up herself. She would deny it strongly unless Peppery directly challenged her on it.

## Authority Distinction

Sho views Peppery as different from Plugg and Scourge.

Plugg and Scourge seem to Sho to delight in malice. Peppery feels cold and dangerous, but not malicious in the same way.

Sho sees Peppery as someone who acts to make sure her captain's will is done. The trust between Peppery and Harrigan also makes Sho see Peppery as a person with a role and relationship, not only as a faceless officer threat.

This does not make Peppery safe. It makes her legible to Sho as competent authority rather than sadistic authority.

## Warning to Sho

Peppery's warning felt kind, dangerous, and unreadable to Sho.

Sho did not read the warning as condescending, though her inexperience may shape that interpretation.

Sho tried to take the warning to heart: choose when to act, and do not always act rashly.

This later informed her restraint on the island when Scourge accused the group of betrayal and Sho had an opportunity to strike, but striking would have carried a high cost.

## Campaign Use

Peppery can be used as part of the observing Harrigan-side officer layer. She registers strangeness, enforces authority, and embodies why Harrigan's command structure works.

For Sho specifically, Peppery helps explain why mutiny had not happened sooner. With people like Peppery and Harrigan in control, people fall in line more easily. Sho does not like that, but she understands it better because of Peppery.

## Relationships

### Sho Astor

Sho is somewhat smitten with Peppery, but embarrassed by it.

Sho views Peppery as competent, cold, beautiful, unreadable, and dangerous. She also sees Peppery as meaningfully different from Plugg and Scourge because Peppery does not appear to delight in malice.

Peppery's warning became part of Sho's developing ability to choose when to act rather than always lashing out the moment a wrong presents itself.

### Rak Wraithraiser

### Barnabas Harrigan

Peppery appears in or near Harrigan's dining / officer context.

Sho reads the trust between Peppery and Harrigan as part of what makes Peppery important and part of why Harrigan's command functions.

### Riaris Krine

Riaris' response contrasts with Peppery's: Peppery notices Rak's strangeness, while Riaris focuses on the useful catch.
