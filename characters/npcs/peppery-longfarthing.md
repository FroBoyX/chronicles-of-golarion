The recovered Erastus 16 reefclaw dinner scene preserves only a small beat for her, but it gives a direct reaction to Rak.

## Identity

- Name: Peppery Longfarthing

## Erastus 16 Reefclaw Dinner

After Rak was brought before Harrigan as the source of the reefclaw dinner and rewarded with the return of his confiscated equipment, Peppery observed:

> "What a strange creature."

Riaris Krine answered the practical side of it, saying he had caught good food and that was good enough for her.

## Campaign Use

Peppery can be used as part of the observing Harrigan-side officer layer. In this scene, she registers Rak's strangeness without becoming the decision-maker.
