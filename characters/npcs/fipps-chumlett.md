# Fipps Chumlett

## Document Status

- Type: NPC dossier
- Maturity: Draft, expanded with Arodus 5 split-scene log
- Scope: Hostile lower-crew NPC, current beach-side loose end

## Overview

Fipps Chumlett is a fat, pushy bully and hostile lower-crew pirate aligned with Plugg and Scourge's side of the shipboard conflict.

At the current relaunch point, Fipps is at Coconut Beach on Bonewrack Isle rather than with Rak's moving supply party.

His separation is now mostly explained: Slippery Syl ordered him to stay at the beach because the ship was expected to meet them there soon.

## Identity

- Name: Fipps Chumlett
- Type: NPC
- Baseline role: Wormwood swab
- Baseline alignment: Neutral Evil
- Current location: Bonewrack Isle, Coconut Beach / south beach rendezvous area
- Current status: Presumed alive, injured or at least worn down from frog/mire fight

## Baseline Adventure Path Role

Fipps is one of the hostile lower-crew pirates used to pressure the PCs early aboard the Wormwood.

He is part of the group that blocks the PCs after dawn under Scourge's encouragement.

If threatened with armed resistance, he is the one most likely to escalate by drawing a dagger in the published scene.

## Campaign Role

Fipps is one of the recurring faces of lower-crew bullying.

He lacks Plugg's authority and Scourge's institutional role, but he participates in the same ecosystem of intimidation.

At the current relaunch point, his role is beach-side: he is left behind while Rak, Syl, Shivikah, and Jape continue toward the stronghold and eventual fork collision.

## Campaign History

### Wormwood Harassment

Fipps was part of the early hostile crew pressure against the PCs.

### Bilges / Aretta Thread

Fipps was present in the orbit of Aretta and other hostile crew during Sho's conflict history.

His precise role in the bilge sequence should be reconstructed from logs.

### Man's Promise and Bonewrack

Fipps transferred into the Man's Promise pressure environment.

He was part of the group sent with Rak for food and water.

During the broken bridge / mire sequence, Fipps was attacked by a frog, flailed, screamed for help, and accidentally stabbed the frog while trying to free himself.

He later appeared visibly out of breath near the cornfield pause.

### Beach Assignment

At Coconut Beach, Rak pointed out the coconut trees in a tour-guide voice.

Syl said they were going to the stronghold and that the ship should be meeting them at the beach soon.

Syl ordered Fipps to stay there.

Everyone else pushed on.

This makes Fipps' current beach location a deliberate assignment by Syl, likely tied to the ship rendezvous, not an unexplained disappearance.

## Current State

- Location: Coconut Beach / south beach rendezvous area
- Status: Presumed alive
- Condition: Worn down, possibly injured or bandaged after frog encounter
- Current role: Left by Syl to wait where the ship should meet the supply party
- Current relevance: May receive the ship, warn returning crew, interfere with boats or supplies, or become isolated if events shift

## Relationships

### Rak Wraithraiser

Rak is not with Fipps at the current moment.

Fipps watched Rak fail or struggle at the broken bridge, and Rak saw Fipps struggle in the frog fight.

### Slippery Syl Lonegan

Syl ordered Fipps to stay at Coconut Beach.

This indicates she still considered him useful but not necessary for the stronghold push.

### Sho Astor

Fipps was part of the hostile pressure environment around Sho.

If he participated directly in prior violence or intimidation against her, that should be recorded.

### Aretta Bansion

Fipps was associated with Aretta and the early hostile group.

Aretta's death may affect his hostility toward Sho or the PCs.

### Plugg and Scourge

Fipps likely aligns with Plugg and Scourge because their order allows him to bully others.

Whether he is loyal or merely opportunistic remains open.

## Campaign Function at Relaunch

Fipps allows Coconut Beach to remain active.

Even if the fork scene becomes the central island collision, the beach still matters because Fipps may:

- meet the ship,
- warn the ship,
- interfere with boats,
- seize or guard coconuts and supplies,
- lie about what happened,
- flee,
- or become a hostage or bargaining piece.

## What Not To Assume

Do not assume Fipps is with Rak's group.

Do not assume his beach position is irrelevant.

Do not assume his loyalty is strong if Plugg's position weakens.
