## GM Note

> Erastus 6th – The new crew members are harassed by Aretta, Syl, Fipps, and Jaundiced at the behest of Master Scourge in order to lay down the law. All four of the harassers end up unconscious and are whipped for being late at bloody hour.

## Short-Form Table Outcome

The second day aboard the ship, a group led by **Slippery Syl Lonegan** was dispatched to send a message to the newer additions to the crew.

An altercation followed.

During the fight:

- **Aretta Bansion** took several blows from **Harvus G'ron**.
- **Mirei** distracted Aretta.
- **Sho Astor** ultimately rendered Aretta unconscious.
- The whole harassment group ended up unconscious:
  - Jaundiced Jape
  - Slippery Syl Lonegan
  - Aretta Bansion
  - Fipps Chumlett

Because the harassers were late to duty, they were whipped at Bloody Hour.

This humiliation further fueled Aretta's need for revenge and points toward the later bilges attack involving Aretta and Fipps.

## Character Descriptors Preserved for Reconstruction

- **Jaundiced Jape:** A humorless, greedy half-orc mute.
- **Fipps Chumlett:** A fat, pushy bully with a shaved head.
- **Slippery Syl Lonegan:** An unhinged murderer who fled to sea to escape the gallows.
- **Aretta Bansion:** A bad-tempered ex-harlot with big ears.
