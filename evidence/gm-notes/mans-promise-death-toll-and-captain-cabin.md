# GM Note — Man's Promise Death Toll and Captain's Cabin Blood

## Document Status

- Type: GM truth / correction note
- Maturity: Canon-setting correction
- Scope: Man's Promise casualty math, Kahina's death-toll statement, and the Man's Promise captain's death location
- Source basis: GM correction in current reconstruction conversation after recovered tactical map review
- Spoiler scope: GM-facing / reconstruction-facing

## Correction

Kahina Bukekayo's statement that more than thirty people died should be considered accurate.

The recovered tactical map confirms the visible / above-deck casualty state and survivor state, but it does **not** account for every death aboard the Man's Promise. It shows who died on the visible battle map and helps place several important events, but it should not be treated as the full shipwide death toll.

To get the final death toll, the archive needs to canonize the Man's Promise crew count.

Known math:

- The recovered map confirms 16 deaths visible / represented on the fight map.
- Those 16 map deaths are 15 Man's Promise-side figures plus Giffer Tibbs.
- The Man's Promise had 15 captured survivors after the battle.
- Harrigan later killed one weakened captured sailor, leaving 14 captured survivors.
- Kahina's "more than thirty" line is accurate because additional deaths beyond the visible / above-deck map count occurred or are implied by the full crew complement.

## Captain's Death Location

The Man's Promise captain was killed in their cabin.

This is supported by later scene evidence: when Mirei went into the captured captain's cabin to perform for Plugg a few nights later, blood was still visible splashed across the floor, desk, and walls.

This means the captain's death should not be folded into the visible deck-map casualty count as if all deaths happened above deck.

## Canon Impact

- Treat the tactical map's 16 deaths as **map-visible / represented deaths**, not the total Man's Promise battle death toll.
- Treat Kahina's "more than thirty" death figure as accurate survivor testimony.
- Treat the captain's death location as the captain's cabin.
- Treat the blood in the captain's cabin during Mirei's later performance as physical evidence of the captain's death.
- Add an open work item to canonize the Man's Promise crew complement and derive the final death toll from crew count minus survivors.
