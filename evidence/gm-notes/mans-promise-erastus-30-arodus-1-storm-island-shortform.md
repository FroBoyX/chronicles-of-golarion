# GM Note — Man's Promise Storm and Island Short-Form Chronology

## Document Status

- Type: GM short-form chronology / table-canon clarification
- Maturity: Current GM truth for storm and first island transition unless contradicted by stronger direct log evidence
- Scope: Erastus 30 through Arodus 1 Man's Promise storm, grindylow attack, shipwreck, water loss, repair estimate, island landing, and Goyle's later separate capture
- Source basis: GM short-form notes and current clarification in archive conversation
- Canon status: Use as table chronology scaffold while tracing raw logs

## GM Short-Form Day Notes

### Erastus 30, 4712 AR

A harsh but normal day of sailing.

### Erastus 31, 4712 AR

A large and sudden storm blasts the Man's Promise.

All hands are needed just to keep her afloat.

The ship is tossed by the waves and driven southwest off course.

During the storm, grindylows attack the ship.

Two sailors are lost at sea during this storm attack:

- Tilly Brackett
- Sandara Quinn

The GM followed the AP storm encounter almost beat-for-beat here: Tilly and Sandara are the sailors taken first, then the grindylows attack openly.

The GM still has the encounter map for this fight.

### Arodus 1, 4712 AR

The Man's Promise is finally blown onto a reef on the shore of an unknown island.

After assessing the situation, the crew determines:

- the fresh water is gone;
- the ship will take two days to repair;
- a team must be sent onto the island to search for food and water.

A team is sent to the island in the late evening to search for food and water.

After the island party sets camp, they are attacked by ghouls in the night.

## Goyle Capture Clarification

Goyle Onórach was **not** one of the two sailors taken during the Erastus 31 storm attack.

The party spends three or more days on the island.

During that period, while the PCs are gone from the Man's Promise, a later / subsequent grindylow attack strikes the ship.

Goyle ends up in the grindylow cavern because he was fighting one of the boarders during that subsequent attack.

Mr. Plugg washed Goyle overboard with an attack from his **Tidal Cutlass** while Goyle was engaged with the boarder.

This means Harvus and Mirei later learn about the Tidal Cutlass' ability from Goyle's account after his rescue.

## Reconstruction Rules

- Treat Sandara Quinn and Tilly Brackett as the two sailors taken in the Erastus 31 storm attack.
- Treat Goyle Onórach's capture as a later, separate grindylow-boarding event while the PCs were on the island.
- Do not merge Goyle's capture into the initial AP-style storm abduction.
- Use the AP scaffold for storm structure, but preserve table-specific names and timing from GM notes and recovered logs.
- Preserve the living-world causality: events continue aboard the Man's Promise while the PCs are on Bonewrack Isle.
- Record Plugg's use of the Tidal Cutlass against Goyle as a key witness fact and as the reason Harvus and Mirei know the weapon's ability.
