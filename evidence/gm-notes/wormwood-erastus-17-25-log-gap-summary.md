This document preserves the current working bridge between the Erastus 16 reef scene and the Erastus 25 Man's Promise battle.

## GM Clarification

Sho's Erastus 24 keelhaul charge was **killing Aretta Bansion on Erastus 23**.

Sho was put in the hot-box on Erastus 24 to be keelhauled at Bloody Hour, but the punishment was not carried out because the Man's Promise was sighted late that afternoon and all preparations shifted to piracy.

In the early morning of Erastus 25, the Wormwood overtook the Man's Promise. Sho was let out because of her combat prowess and was given the opportunity to lead a strike team in the battle.

## Timeline

### Erastus 17 — Heatwave Begins

A heatwave hits the Wormwood.

### Erastus 18 — Heatwave Continues

The heatwave refuses to let up as the boiling sun beats across the water.

### Erastus 19 — Boarding Drills

The weather lets up.

Captain Harrigan assigns Riaris Krine to put everyone through boarding drills in rotations.

### Erastus 20 — Normal Work Day

A normal day of work aboard the Wormwood.

### Erastus 21 — Normal Work Day

A normal day of work aboard the Wormwood.

### Erastus 22 — Plugg Watches Sho

Mr. Plugg takes notice of Sho Astor's defiance.

He takes note of who the monk interacts with.

Plugg also takes it as his personal task to ensure anyone who has shown defiance gets corrected.

### Erastus 23 — Bilges, Fipps, Aretta, and Sho

Mr. Plugg assigns Sho to the bilges with Aretta Bansion and Fipps Chumlett.

The working understanding is that Aretta and Fipps are placed there with orders to make sure Sho cannot continue being an issue.

Aretta does not survive the ensuing fight.

### Erastus 24 — Hot-Box and Sails

Sho is put in the hot-box under the charge of killing Aretta and is to be keelhauled at Bloody Hour.

In the late afternoon, the Man's Promise is sighted.

All preparations are made for piracy, and the keelhauling is not carried out.

### Erastus 25 — Battle for the Man's Promise

In the early morning, the Wormwood overtakes the Man's Promise.

Sho is released because of her combat prowess and given the opportunity to lead a strike team.

All hands, including Sho, take part in a bloody and violent battle for control of the ship.
