# Sho Astor Rum-Ration Refusal

## Document Status

- Type: GM truth / character note
- Maturity: Canon note
- Source: GM reconstruction note during archive rebuild
- Scope: Sho Astor's recurring Wormwood behavior around rum ration
- Canon status: Treat as true unless later direct log evidence contradicts method/details

## Canon Note

Sho Astor never drank her rum ration aboard the Wormwood.

We do not currently have direct evidence of how she got rid of it each night.

The current reconstruction is that she consistently disposed of, hid, traded, poured out, or otherwise avoided consuming the ration.

## Usage Rule

Do not write Sho as willingly drinking the Wormwood rum unless later evidence directly contradicts this.

It is acceptable to write scenes where the rum ration is distributed, enforced, or socially expected, but Sho's participation should be framed as quiet refusal or concealed avoidance rather than consumption.
