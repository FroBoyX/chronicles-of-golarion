## Extracted Facts

### Wormwood Role

Aretta had been aboard the Wormwood for about five months.

She was a trusted goon of Master Scourge and took on tasks set before her by Scourge and Mr. Plugg when they needed some degree of separation from the act.

### Press-Ganging Harvus

On the night Harvus was beaten and brought aboard the Wormwood, Aretta was one of five pirates involved.

She stole from Harvus and others who were gang-pressed that night.

### Day 2 Message / Early Harassment

On the second day aboard the ship, a group led by Slippery Syl Lonegan was dispatched to send a message to the newer additions to the crew.

The group included:

- Jaundiced Jape
- Slippery Syl Lonegan
- Aretta Bansion
- Fipps Chumlett

An altercation followed.

Aretta took several blows from Harvus, was distracted by Mirei, and was ultimately rendered unconscious by Sho Astor.

### Day 2 Bloody Hour

The whole group was whipped for showing up to duty late.

This fills the Day 2 Bloody Hour beat: the goons were punished, not the new hands.

This further fueled Aretta's need for revenge.

### Later Bilge Duty and Death

A few days later, Fipps and Aretta were assigned bilge duty with Sho.

Fipps and Aretta brandished weapons and attacked Sho.

Sho, not knowing her own strength, beat Aretta within an inch of her life.

Aretta died soon after from complications because medical attention was slow to come.

## Reconstruction Implications

- Aretta is the dead attacker in Sho's bilges memory.
- The Day 2 hostile-crew encounter is not the same as the later bilges ambush/death.
- Day 2 Bloody Hour should show Syl, Jape, Aretta, and Fipps being whipped for lateness after the failed intimidation/harassment job.
- Aretta's later attack on Sho should be framed as revenge fueled by prior humiliation and punishment.
- Sho's guilt around Aretta should include both direct violence and the ship's delayed/poor medical response.
