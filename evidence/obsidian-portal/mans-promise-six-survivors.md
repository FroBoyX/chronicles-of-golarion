## Reconstruction Rules

- Treat these six as the named tracked Man's Promise survivor set.
- Do not treat Ebony Saable as merely an assumed survivor; he is part of this confirmed set.
- Do not assume all six are in the same physical place at the relaunch moment:
  - Goyle is confirmed away from the ship, traveling with Harvus, Mirei, and Tilly after the Riptide Cove rescue.
  - Kahina is aboard the Man's Promise and interacting with Sho.
  - The exact current positions of Dahrehn, Ebony, Merchiel, and Nor'gal should be confirmed, but they belong to the survivor set.
- Do not assume loyalty or forgiveness from survivor status. These survivors may oppose Plugg while still distrusting Sho and the other Wormwood pirates.

## Relaunch Use

This set should anchor the Man's Promise survivor bloc during the hot-box / mutiny / legitimacy sequence.

The group may matter for:

- whether Sho is released;
- whether Kahina can act with backing;
- whether Goyle has survivor authority when he returns;
- whether the ship can still be crewed after conflict;
- whether the survivors accept, reject, or conditionally tolerate any new captain;
- and whether Sho's guilt can be tested against named people rather than an abstract dead crew.
