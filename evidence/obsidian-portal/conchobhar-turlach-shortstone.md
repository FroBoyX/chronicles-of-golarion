## Description

Conchobhar Turlach Shortstone is a handsome, honey-tongued gnome who wears a foppish purple hat, an eye patch, and a white silk shirt, and carries a dandyish cane.

His appearance is that of someone pretending to be a pirate, rather than a true buccaneer.

## Identity

- Race: Gnome
- Gender: Male

## Background

Conchobhar is a professional gambler who made the mistake of drinking with the Wormwood's crew one night and failed to notice the cosh in a crew member's hand until it was too late.

He has been on ship less than 24 hours at the start of the adventure.

Conchobhar is resigned to his fate but is determined to make the most of it, as he has always yearned for the sea anyway.

One thing dominates Conchobhar's thinking: himself.

Conchobhar is also a serial womanizer.

## Combat / Morale

Conchobhar prefers to talk rather than fight. If he must fight, he lurks at the edge of combat, preferring to flank or attack with his bow.

Conchobhar flees if reduced to 7 hit points or fewer, but he fights to the death for any female character he is on friendly or better terms with.

Female characters gain a +2 bonus on all checks to influence him.

## Additional Notes

- Conchobhar found Rosie Cusswell magnificent.
- Conchobhar was one of the press-ganged new hands and took part in the early Wormwood trials.
- He became a rigger.
- He quickly faded into the background as an NPC after the first few days.
