## Description

Crimson "Cog" Cogward is a gravely serious but devout sailor who is quick to start a fight, and turns dark red when angry.

Crimson Cogward is so called after the color his neck turns when he is angry.

## Identity / Bearing

Cog is a loner, prone to incredible rages and dark moods. He wears a blue Varisian scarf tied tight around his shaven head to hide the scar left when his ear was bitten off in a bar fight.

Cog gets pleasure from little other than combat and is quick to get into fistfights, whether because of a chance remark or simple bad luck.

Cog is a devout follower of Pharasma and prays regularly, particularly after combat. He participates in all Pharasmin religious festivals if possible.

## Wormwood Entry

Cog volunteered to join the crew of the Wormwood.

Current GM clarification: Crimson was part of the early Wormwood trials with the press-ganged new hands, but he was not press-ganged.

Cog seems to have a way of acquiring items and goods for barter and trade.

## GM Only

### Combat

Cog fights like a cornered boar, caring little for his own safety.

### Morale

Cog does not fight to lose. He fights to win, or dies trying.

### Influence Hooks

Cog loves dark tales. A character who makes at least a DC 10 Perform (oratory) check focusing on dark tales gains a +2 bonus on checks to influence Cog for the next 24 hours.

Fighters and other warriors, as well as aggressive characters who favor muscle over talk, can gain a +2 bonus on Bluff and Diplomacy checks with him by interspersing conversation with grisly details of fights.

A failed check is likely to elicit a violent response from the angry sailor.
