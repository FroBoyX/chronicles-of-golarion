- Mirei marks Day 16 and remains badly injured from the prior punishment sequence.
- Mirei privately tells the GM she will try to influence Giffer Tibbs and rolls 21.
- Giffer warms slightly to Mirei.

- Sho asks whether anything can help Mirei recover, and Mirei says she does not think so.
- Mirei asks Sho whether Sho has made progress making friends; Sho says not yet but may try that night.
- Mirei performs with a roll of 22.

### Day 16 — Peppery Warns Sho

- Sho tries to talk with Peppery Longfarthing while Peppery is on duty.
- Peppery tells Sho to spend leisure time elsewhere and then gives stronger advice: when someone is busy or gives an order, Sho should not insist on her own way.
- Peppery explicitly says she has patience but others do not, and warns Sho to learn discipline before the whip teaches it.
- This sharpens Peppery's role as stern, non-sadistic, and disciplined rather than safe.

### Day 17 — Plugg Takes Over Assignments and Punishment

- Plugg calls all hands on deck.
- He announces that due to discipline issues, he will make all crew assignments going forward.
- Plugg also says he will oversee punishment and personally check task progress around the ship.
- He says the Wormwood left the Shackles the previous day and is sailing south toward merchants and plunder.
- Plugg assigns Mirei to runner duty and says he will take it easy on her.
- Plugg assigns Harvus to the bilges, claiming it is to keep his wing safe.
- Plugg assigns Sho to rat-catching / infestation duty below deck because she has proven useful with her hands.
- Plugg checks Rak's rum compliance through Kroop/Ambrose.

### Day 17 — Conchobhar Avoids Mirei

- Mirei privately rolls 25 Diplomacy for Conchobhar and approaches him after he attended her performance the prior night.
- Conchobhar says he likes Mirei, but she hangs around the monk and he does not want to be associated with the PCs.
- He has heard things and says it is not pretty.
- Mirei apologizes and returns to work.
- This is strong evidence that after the mast punishments and Sho/Harvus trouble, association with the PCs begins socially contaminating Mirei.

### Day 17 — Sandara Warns Harvus About Polarization

- Harvus asks Sandara whether she has heard why Plugg pulled him from rigging and assigned him to the bilges.
- Sandara warns that people are starting to avoid association with Harvus and Sho because of the attention they drew the other night.
- She says things are getting polarized on the Wormwood.
- Sandara says she is trying to remain neutral so she can watch all of their backs.
- This broadens the Day 15 Sandara warning: visible factional alignment is becoming dangerous.

### Day 17 — Ratline / Mirei Friendly Contact

- This reinforces Ratline as receptive to Mirei's performances and adds him to her social map.

### Day 17 — Owlbear / Sho Patience Lesson

- Owlbear thanks her and warns she should be more careful with her words.
- He says he could break his chains if he needed to, but there is no need; he gets food and bides his time.
- Sho recognizes that he is wiser than she assumed.
- This reinforces Owlbear as patient captive rather than helpless brute, and mirrors the broader theme of biding time.

### Day 17 — Rak / Tilly / Crimson Arm-Wrestling

- Rak uses his strength/mutagenic edge and wins 20 gp from Crimson.
- Tilly is impressed and later suggests she and Rak could make money together: she suckers people in, Rak knocks them down.
- Rak explains that he dislikes the ship's rum, and Tilly realizes this is why he altered drinks earlier.
- This continues the Rak/Tilly gambling partnership and sets up the next day's con.

- Riaris warns that fog will limit visibility and tells Sho to stick to orders.
- Sho is allowed to choose a team and is ordered to report back in ten minutes.
- This gives concrete evidence for why Sho was released from the box and why she was in a command role during the Man's Promise boarding.

- This is a valuable private-perspective scene: Mirei checked on Sho just before the Man's Promise boarding and knew Sho might survive the hot-box/keelhauling situation through some plan or condition.

### Day 20 / 21 — Sho Assembles Team

- Sho recruits Harvus, who agrees immediately.
- Sho tries to recruit Crimson, who refuses and assumes Harvus may have let her out.
- Sho recruits Giffer; Giffer tells Sho that if she has officer orders, she should stop asking and start ordering.
- Sho tries to recruit Owlbear, but Owlbear says he will be with Master Plugg.
- Harvus tries to recruit Sandara, but Sandara is assigned to Scourge's boarding party under Plugg's order.
- Sho eventually asks Mirei to join because the fog makes the situation feel wrong and she would rather have Mirei close than separated.
- Mirei agrees and gets a dagger from Grok because no shortsword or rapier is available.
- Grok also gives Mirei a scroll and tells her to be careful.
- Rak says goodbye to Grok before boarding, and she helps arm him / support him.

- Rak drops the pigs.
- Riaris checks whether Sho's group is ready.
