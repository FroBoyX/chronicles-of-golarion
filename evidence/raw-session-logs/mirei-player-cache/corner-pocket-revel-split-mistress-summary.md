## High-Value Reconstruction Extractions

### Erastus 27 Revel / Party Setup

- The log begins aboard the Wormwood during the post-Man's Promise victory party.
- Harrigan has met with top officers and divided plunder from the Man's Promise.
- The party is in full swing with fine food and drink from the Man's Promise hold.
- Many officers join the revelry.
- Harvus, Sho, and Mirei are still wounded and recovering.

### Riaris Rewards Sho's Boarding Team

- Riaris approaches Sho and says Sho's team performed poorly in some ways but got the job done.
- Riaris gives Sho a large sack and orders her to distribute the loot among the team.
- Riaris notes that the team lost some reward for losing a member and tells Sho to be more careful next time.
- The chat notes `1100 gp` and `4 potions` in connection with the team reward.
- Sho gives Harvus his share and one cure light wounds potion when he asks to keep one on hand.
- This is a clean source for the team reward and Sho acting as distributor.

### Harrigan Rewards Sho

- Harrigan summons Sho to his cabin.
- Plugg is present and leaves at Harrigan's order, scowling and warning Sho as he passes.
- Harrigan says that eighteen hours earlier Sho had been headed under his boat, and now he is giving her a reward.
- Harrigan gives Sho a sack and says Plugg has been ordered to lay off her because she is under Harrigan's protection.
- Harrigan gives Sho an amulet as thanks for warning him about the attacker during boarding.
- Harrigan says the sack contains an extra 200 gp and that the amulet should keep her safe in future encounters.
- Harrigan says Plugg thinks he should not trust Sho, but Sho showed true colors during the fight.
- Harrigan adds that the officer Sho captured provided information after she stopped screaming and says he instructed Grok to give Sho another 200 gp for that.
- Harrigan then warns Sho not to mistake support for friendship: Plugg represents Harrigan, and future disobedience will be dealt with personally.
- This sharpens Sho's Harrigan-grey thread: reward and protection come with a direct threat.

### Sho / Scourge After Reward

- Sho approaches the locked hatch / captive area and Scourge confronts her.
- Sho says she was observing the captives and not partying.
- Scourge says the hold is off limits to Sho's kind, captain's favor or not, under Plugg's orders.
- Scourge says he was impressed by the bodies Sho's team left and frames the demoralized prisoners as proper.
- Sho says she did not intend to kill as many as they did and will not in future, though she will get the job done.
- Scourge advises that at sea it is kill or be killed.
- Sho says it does not have to be and leaves.
- This is a direct philosophical conflict after the boarding: Sho's restraint versus Scourge's sea-law cruelty.

### Sho Protects Fallen Mirei During Revel

- Sho sees drunk crew crowding over a fallen Mirei and tells them to get away.
- Crew including Maheem, Crimson, Patch, Jape, Tam, and others are present around the situation.
- Some claim to be checking Mirei's pulse, but Sho notices inappropriate rummaging / hands in robes and challenges them.
- Conchobhar says Mirei needs air more than anything.
- Rak distracts or draws attention by calling for a party, then picks Mirei up and takes her down to the hammocks.
- Tam intervenes physically, throws Sho to the ground, and stands between Sho and the escalating crew, framing the party as not a fight and warning that at sea they do not hold grudges.
- Patch threatens to report Sho to Plugg / Scourge, while Maheem threatens Sho on land.
- Sho leaves wounded and angry but de-escalates.
- This is important Sho/Mirei/Rak/Tam evidence: Sho tries to protect Mirei, Rak removes Mirei from the dangerous scene, and Tam prevents the fight from becoming open violence.

### Harvus Watches Over Sandara

- Sandara wakes after being unconscious or badly injured and asks Harvus how long she has been out.
- Harvus says roughly thirty hours and that he stayed because her wound was grievous.
- Harvus says he has not seen Giffer since the fight and fears she was lost.
- Sandara tells Harvus not to let the lifestyle take away his sweetness.
- Harvus eventually goes to rest.

### Erastus 28 / Harrigan Announces Ship Split

- The post-boarding party lasts roughly thirty-six hours and winds down before dawn.
- Harrigan calls all hands on deck and announces that the Wormwood now has a second ship but only one crew.
- The Wormwood will sail onward with new shipmates, while Plugg picks a skeleton crew to sail the Man's Promise to Port Peril for salvage.
- Harrigan says some captured crew may be worth ransom from families in Azir, while the rest can either become pirates or spend their lives at sea.
- Harrigan throws one weakened sailor overboard, and some Wormwood crew cheer.
- Several Man's Promise sailors grovel and beg to join the crew.
- Harrigan assigns the doomed souls to Plugg and tells him to make a crew for the Promise.
- This is a strong source for the split structure and the coercive recruitment of Man's Promise survivors.

### Transfer To Plugg's Skeleton Crew

- Plugg orders Sho to report to the poop deck of the Man's Promise in ten minutes.
- Scourge orders Harvus to the deck of the Man's Promise.
- Kroop tells Rak to help pack because they are heading with the new ship to Port Peril.
- Grok does not come with Rak, holds back tears, and says she has a present for him before he goes.
- Plugg tells Mirei he needs personal entertainment to keep morale up and orders her to report to the new ship.
- Mirei goes to get her things from her trunk, and Sho moves with her.
- This confirms the PC transfer to Plugg's skeleton crew and Rak/Grok separation.

### Plugg's First Man's Promise Address

- Plugg tells the transferred sailors to address him as Captain Plugg.
- Scourge is first mate.
- Syl is boatswain and her orders carry Plugg's authority.
- Plugg claims the task is to return to Port Peril and then to the Wormwood.
- Scourge warns that the Wormwood took it easy on them and that discipline will be dealt with quickly and forcefully on the skeleton crew.
- Syl tells everyone to keep their Wormwood jobs and threatens the mast under her care.
- This establishes the Man's Promise command structure.
