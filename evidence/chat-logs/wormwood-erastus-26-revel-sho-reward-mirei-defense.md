## Reconstruction Use

Use this as the primary evidence for the post-Man's Promise revel and the transition into the prisoner muster / prize-crew split.

Key functions:

- Sho is rewarded and protected by Harrigan, but not forgiven into safety.
- Plugg is explicitly told to lay off Sho, which explains why his resentment becomes more dangerous rather than less.
- Sho's moral stance is repeated three times: she regrets casualties, rejects needless killing, and intervenes when Mirei is vulnerable.
- Tam's pirate-custom intervention is direct evidence for the distinction between shipboard order and land grudges.
- Rak directly protects Mirei by physically removing her from the revel danger.
- Sandara's 30-hour recovery explains why Harvus has been focused on her during the celebration.
- The party length sets the transition into the Erastus 27 bell / prisoner muster.
