## Extracted Facts

- The preserved fragment begins after all hands have already been called on deck and while the fight is already underway.
- Initiative values visible in the fragment include Harvus at 24, Mirei at 21, and Rak at 8.
- Harvus is shown at 24 / 25 HP after gaining 2 HP.
- Rak takes repeated damage during the fragment, dropping from 20 maximum HP to 14, then 12, then 8, then 2 / 20 HP, before being healed back up to 9 and later 17 / 20 HP.
- The fight is happening on the `Man's Promise` map.
- Sho and Rak appear to be on deck fighting grindylows together.
- Harvus is up in the rigging during at least part of the fight and feels something pierce the sails.
- The GM note suggests Harvus' comment may refer to a grindylow head being flung into the sails.
- Mirei falls from the rigging during the storm.
- Sho runs toward where Mirei is going to land and cushions / catches her fall enough that Mirei thanks Sho and gets up.
- Sho later tells Harvus that she and Rak fought a bunch of grindylows on deck.
- Sho says the grindylows seemed especially interested in Rak.
- Harvus says it sounds like he missed some action, reinforcing that he was separated from the main deck fight by rigging work or position.

## Reconstruction Use

This fragment confirms that the open grindylow fight was not only abstract AP scaffold: PCs were actively on the Man's Promise map during the storm, with Rak taking heavy damage, Sho fighting on deck, Harvus in the rigging, and Mirei falling from the rigging in storm conditions.

It also gives an early storm-scene rescue beat between Sho and Mirei: Sho physically moves to cushion Mirei's fall and Mirei thanks her. Do not overstate injury, exact mechanics, or emotional fallout beyond the visible exchange.

The fragment supports that grindylows were particularly focused on Rak, but does not explain why.
