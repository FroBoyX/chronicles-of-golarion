## Reconstruction Use

Use this as the first formal public command scene aboard the Man's Promise.

The scene establishes the initial prize-crew hierarchy:

- Plugg claims captaincy.
- Scourge is first mate.
- Slippery Syl is boatswain.
- Kroop transfers as cook / practical galley support.
- Sho, Harvus, Rak, and Mirei are all ordered onto the Man's Promise.
- Grok remains behind on the Wormwood, creating Rak's separation beat.

This speech is important because Plugg's public mission is still **Port Peril and return to the Wormwood**, even though later evidence shows the Man's Promise changes course.
