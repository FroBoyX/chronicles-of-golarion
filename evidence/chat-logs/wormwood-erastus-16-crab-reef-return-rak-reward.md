## AP Context Summary

The AP scaffold sends the PCs plus one friendly NPC to a nearby reef to gather crabs for Harrigan's supper. Reefclaws attack at the reef. Reefclaw meat can fill a crab pot. Bringing reefclaw meat back for Harrigan can trigger a reward: return of one PC's confiscated equipment, or a potion if that character already recovered everything.

In this table version, the selected group was Harvus, Rak, Sho, Mirei, and Badger. Rak appears to have brought back the notable reefclaw / "big one" and was taken to Harrigan as the source of dinner.

- Caulky brought Rak and the dinner to Harrigan.
- Harrigan rewarded Rak by instructing Grok to return anything taken from him from ship stores.
- Harrigan told Rak he smiles on those who go above and beyond.
- Harrigan offered future money and infamy for doing right by him.

- Plugg dismissed Rak and warned him not to get caught throwing out rum.
- Peppery called Rak a strange creature.
- Riaris judged the result good enough because Rak caught good food.
