## Extracted Facts

- Samms woke Harvus and spoke nervously after looking around at the others sleeping.
- Samms asked whether Sandara had spoken to Harvus that morning.
- Harvus said Sandara had briefly mentioned the altered course.
- Samms said the course had been subtly changing all day / night and that Sandara said they were going east.
- Harvus understood that as back out to sea rather than toward port.
- Samms suspected the ship might be heading toward Bloodcove.
- Harvus whispered that Plugg and Scourge were probably up to something.
- Samms did not like their prospects and noted that Plugg's side had claimed they would follow the captain's orders and sail to Port Peril, but the course changed one day in.
- Samms knew a fair number of people hated Sho / "that monk."
- Samms explicitly trusted Harvus and Sandara.
- Samms wanted Harvus to keep his eyes open and see whether they could get a few more solid allies.
- Later, Kroop asked to talk to Rak privately in the galley.
- Kroop began to ask Rak to close the door, then noticed Sandara and invited her in with relief.
- Kroop said Harrigan was something else, but Plugg was not worth his salt: a lap-dog with insecure delusions of grandeur.
- Kroop did not expect Plugg to follow orders once out of Harrigan's earshot and eyesight.
- Sandara had been calculating the course change all day and confirmed they were certainly going east.
- Sandara said there were many crew whispers, dangerous talk, but that they needed more information first.
- Kroop inferred the likely destination as Rickety Squibs.
- Sandara did not know Rickety Squibs by name.
- Kroop said Rickety Squibs was west of Bloodcove and even more notorious.
- Sandara identified Bloodcove as a notorious port on the edge of the Mwangi Expanse, suitable for bloodthirsty crews or people running from something.
- Kroop explained Rickety Squibs as an isolated dry dock west of Bloodcove where Rickety Hake refits ships into squibs, changing rigging, shape, and features until they are unrecognizable.
- Sandara concluded that if Plugg was going there, he really had betrayed Barnabas Harrigan.
- Sandara told Rak and Kroop to keep it between them, trust no one, and said she would keep an ear out.
- Sandara had hoped they were going back to port.

## Reconstruction Use

This scene moves the course-change thread from suspicion to a working political conclusion.

Sandara has been calculating the course all day, Samms is already seeking allies, and Kroop identifies the likely destination as Rickety Squibs. This makes Plugg's betrayal of Harrigan legible before Bonewrack, even if the crew does not yet have enough information to act openly.

The scene also clarifies early anti-Plugg coordination channels:

- Sandara tracks the course.
- Samms warns Harvus and thinks in terms of allies.
- Kroop interprets the destination and Plugg's likely betrayal.
- Rak receives the Rickety Squibs explanation and is told to keep quiet.
