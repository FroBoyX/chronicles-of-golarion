## Extracted Facts

- Maheem spoke with Harvus during work, likely while both were in or near the rigging.
- Maheem initially sneered at Harvus, saying Harvus did not put up much of a fight when brought aboard.
- Maheem had seen what Harvus did in the Day 2 fight belowdecks.
- Maheem distanced himself from Harvus' wing injury / mistreatment, saying it was not him.
- Maheem said Scourge was responsible.
- Maheem confirmed that Maheem and others dragged Harvus aboard and dumped him.
- Maheem said Scourge stated Harvus needed to be "tamed."

## Reconstruction Use

This scene likely belongs on Erastus 6 during the workday after the morning harassment fight.

It should be used as an early Harvus/Maheem rigging beat and as evidence that:

- news of the morning fight spread through the crew quickly;
- Maheem was involved in bringing Harvus aboard, but distinguished that from Scourge's cruelty;
- Scourge's treatment of Harvus was explicitly framed as "taming" him;
- Harvus' damaged wing and humiliation were part of Scourge's deliberate dominance behavior, not incidental rough handling.
