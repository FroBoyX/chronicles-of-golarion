## Extracted Facts

- Mirei used some kind of distance communication, likely the **message** spell / cantrip, during the Rosie rescue attempt.
- Mirei told Rosie to hold on.
- Mirei told Rosie that the crew or rescuers were trying to get to her.
- Mirei instructed Rosie to tie the rope to herself.
- Mirei repeated the instruction to tie the rope to herself the next time it reached her.
- Mirei told Rosie, "We'll save you."
- The fragments indicate an active rescue attempt involving a rope reaching or trying to reach Rosie.
- The fragments do not themselves confirm who threw the rope, who was physically in the water, or the final mechanical reason the rescue failed.

## Reconstruction Use

Use this as the recovered table evidence that Rosie was not simply lost without attempted intervention.

Mirei was actively communicating with Rosie across distance during the storm, trying to keep her calm and instructing her to secure a rope.

This should not be over-expanded into a successful or nearly successful rescue unless later evidence supports that. The only direct facts here are the messages and the rope instruction.
