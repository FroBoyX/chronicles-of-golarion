## Key Dialogue Extracts

### Harp Return

Mirei asks:

> "Can i has that back?"

Sandara asks:

> "Oh, is this yours?"

Sandara produces the harp.

Mirei confirms:

> "Yes it is"

Sandara says:

> "You'll probably want that."

This confirms that Sandara returned or handed over Mirei's harp very early aboard the Wormwood.

### Sandara's Early Warning

Sandara explains her intent:

> "Anyway, you should keep your friends close aboard ship. I just thought I could help out."

She warns:

> "Try to keep your head down, and avoid the attention of Scurge and Plugg."

She adds:

> "If you need anything, just ask.... and don't start anything you can't finish."

This establishes Sandara as early helper, practical warning-giver, and someone who understands the ship's danger.

### Harvus Introduces Courtly Flirtation

Harvus says:

> "Mi lady, your beauty is boundles. It's as if Calistria herself hath transformed corporeal!"

Sandara responds to Mirei's introduction and Harvus' compliment together:

> "Hey Mirei, I'm Sandara. *Chuckles at the bird* You know, that's not the first time I've been told that... happens here in the shakles."

This is an early Harvus/Sandara tone-setting beat: Harvus is elaborate and reverent; Sandara is amused, practiced, and not easily overwhelmed by praise.

### Harvus Names Himself

Later, Sandara says:

> "Well hello again, I didn't catch your name last time."

Harvus answers:

> "Harvus G'ron milady."

Sandara offers a hand and asks:

> "What can I do for you?"

Harvus kisses her knuckles.

### Harvus' Wing

Sandara asks:

> "That wing of yours okay?"

Harvus answers:

> "ugh... some knave broke it. Is there a doctor aboard?"

Sandara identifies the Stitchman:

> "The “Stitchman”."

She clarifies:

> "He is the carpanter... you might want to avoid him."

Harvus asks whether there are healing potions aboard.

Sandara says:

> "Well, I have a few, nothing that would cure that though... sorry to say, you may be living with that for awhile."

She offers practical care:

> "I'll see what I can do to make sure it sets properly. How is the pain?"

After Harvus thanks her, she says:

> "I'll see if I can work something out for that... maybe a sling."

This is important early evidence that Sandara recognized the wing injury, could not simply cure it, and offered care rather than dismissal.

### Grok / Quartermaster Advice

Harvus asks:

> "I would inquire into the quartermaster? I wish to retrieve my items. How do you see best to approach her?"

Sandara says:

> "Well, I'll give you a little information about her.."

She explains:

> "She is just a bit superstisius... if you can convince her that something you want is cursed she might just give it to you. That's how I got that harp back."

She adds the specific trick:

> "*Winks* I told her it was cursed by the sea... and only I could clense it.... but the owner would have to regularly play it so that the ship wouldn't be sunk."

Sandara gives two more approaches:

> "Or, you could give her alcohal... she is always looking for things other then rum to drink."

and:

> "She is also quite fond of music, so that can curry some favor."

This confirms the harp-return scheme: Sandara used Grok's superstition, sea-curse logic, and Mirei's regular playing as the explanation for why the harp needed to be returned.

### Giffer Reacts

After Harvus thanks Sandara and departs or closes the exchange, Giffer Tibbs says:

> "Well he's cute."

Sandara responds:

> "Oh. *Chuckles* I suppose."

This is a small but useful crew-social beat. It shows Harvus' style being noticed by others and Sandara treating the flirtation with amused restraint.
