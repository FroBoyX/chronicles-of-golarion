## Raw Chat

```text
*Merie knocks*
Master Scourge:	*Opens door* Welcome back, "Captains" wench. *Walks out*
Mr Plugg:	Good, now then. What is tonights entertainment,
	Mirei:	I think I'll play the fiddle again if you don't mind.
Mr Plugg:	I will accept that... prepear a song for the morrow.
Mirei:	What kind of song would you like for tomorrow?
Mr Plugg:	We will cross that bridge IF it comes. *Listens to the fiddle*
	Mr Plugg:	A good performance.... but not as good as yesterday. *Pauses*
	Mr Plugg:	So... that brings us to that "unpleasent" wench business.
	Mr Plugg:	If you "agree" to be my entertainer I would have you stay here, you won't have to work as a swab and you would be treated with the respect of an officer on the mans promise...
	Mr Plugg:	You may speak...
Mirei:	I wouldn't mind still helping around the ship a little. I feel like if I didn't I would lose respect... or that the respect I would be given would be sarcastic and fake.
Mirei:	I do like entertaining though.
Mr Plugg:	You will earn respect, with your voice and presence... not the labor, for I will raise you in station along with me as a lady of a pirate lord but you must sing our deeds. *Voice goes cold* I need an answer...
	Mirei:	Ok... I agree.
Mr Plugg:	Good... only I will refer to you as wench. For the rest, you will need a name. Come up with one by the morning...
	Mr Plugg:	The captain of this ship was a woman... her clothing... and stuff are yours. I will not tolerate ANY disobediance... but should you do my bidding, you will earn the respect of my crew and all who hear of our exploits!
	Mr Plugg:	ALSO.... if you do anything wrong... I will give you to scourge, to do with as he pleases. Until then, you have my protection. Are we clear.
	Mirei:	Yes... Thats very clear.
Mr Plugg:	I will expect your new name in the morning. Bed, now.
```

## Reconstruction Use

This is a central Plugg / Mirei coercion scene.

It clarifies that Plugg's offer is not simply better quarters or patronage. It is a forced social reclassification: entertainer, protected possession, officer-adjacent symbol, singer of Plugg's future deeds, and renamed figure under his authority.

The former female captain's clothing and belongings become part of the coercion. Plugg is not merely giving Mirei gear; he is trying to overwrite her station and attach her visibly to his claim over the ship.

The threat to hand Mirei to Scourge if she disobeys is explicit. Plugg's protection is conditional and proprietary.
