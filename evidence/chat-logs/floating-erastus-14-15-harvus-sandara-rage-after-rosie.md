Harvus:	To Sandara: My queen, I seek your aid.

	Sandara Quinn:	*She places a hand on his shoulder* Is the wing any better... *she sighs a moment* I fear you'll want that fear Harvus. Keep it... we may have to use it. Things are getting worse on the Wormwood. Rosie was new here, but she angered scourge and Plug pretty quick. I was busy in the rigging during the storm... but her going over, it might have been intentional....
	Sandara Quinn:	...and me and scourge don't see eye to eye... keep your rage inside, and your friends close, there may be a time we need to use it. For now, rest your heart, and your wing.
	Sandara Quinn:	I'll pray for besmara to look over us.
	Harvus:	My, I sully mine spirits to place such a burden on you but I feel you are the vessel which restrains my rage.
	Harvus:	*my queen.
	Sandara Quinn:	*Pats him again before going onto deck at the call* Then I shall attempt to see it to good use when the time comes. Just call me Sandara though, queen is just a little too formal for this gal, okay. *Smiles weakly and heads up*
	Harvus:	Good night Sandara.
