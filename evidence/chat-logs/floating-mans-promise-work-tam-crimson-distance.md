## Reconstruction Use

Use this scene as evidence that the Man's Promise transfer did not create a clean social reset for the PCs.

Some crew understood the Man's Promise as a new start, but also saw association with the PCs, especially Sho's defiance, as dangerous. Tam and Crimson choose civil distance rather than open friendship.

This scene also supports Crimson as socially self-protective: he may not hate Mirei or Harvus personally, but he will avoid them if association creates risk.
