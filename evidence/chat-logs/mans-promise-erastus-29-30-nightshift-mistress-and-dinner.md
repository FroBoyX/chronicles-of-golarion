## Reconstruction Use

These scenes show the next stage of Mirei's coerced status becoming visible to the crew.

Plugg tries to make her "Mistress Mirei" into a symbol of his authority: dressed in the dead captain's clothing, wearing jewelry and a jeweled sword as his sign, eating captain's food, and being sent below to test crew loyalty.

The crew does not receive the new station cleanly. Sho is concerned. Sandara plays along while privately maneuvering. Tam openly challenges whether Mirei has any claim to respect or authority.

This also gives a strong pre-Bonewrack evidence chain for Sandara trying to confirm the ship's destination before open crisis.
