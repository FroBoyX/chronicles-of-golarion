## Extracted Facts

- Rak returned to Grok at night after earlier failing to stop by.

- Grok was willing to open the shop for Rak.
- Grok remembered that Rak had mentioned alcohol.
- Rak said he needed ingredients and his kit to brew.
- Rak claimed he could brew almost anything with the kit and ingredients.
- Rak used the phrase / table joke, "I put on my robe and wizard hat."
- Grok responded positively and invited him to show what he could brew.
- GM reconstruction presumes Grok furnished Rak's alchemy kit and ingredients.
- This scene is the likely beginning of Rak and Grok's friendship, eventually leading into their later intimate relationship.
