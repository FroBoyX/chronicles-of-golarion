## Scene Candidate

**Rak makes Grok some alcohol**

## GM Note

It is presumed that Grok furnished both Rak's alchemy kit and ingredients to brew with.

This is the first recovered explicit friendship-development scene between Rak and Grok. It should not be treated as their first contact. Given Rak's cook's mate duties under Ambrose Kroop, Grok's closeness to Kroop, and the AP's heavy galley / quartermaster context, it is easy to assume Rak and Grok had already crossed paths earlier through galley work or duties for Ambrose. Those earlier interactions are not currently recovered.

## Raw Chat

```text
Cut-Throat Grok:	Well hello again, were you looking for something from the shop? *Smiles, if you can call it that*
	Rak Wraithraiser:	Nah brah, just wanted to apologize for bailing earlier. I meant to stop by but I lost track of time while cooking up the grub.
	Cut-Throat Grok:	No worries, I could open it up for you now if you'd like. You mentioned alcohol?
	Rak Wraithraiser:	Heck yeah, I just need some ingredients to make the brew and my kit and I can brew up pretty much anything.
	Rak Wraithraiser:	I put on my robe and wizard hat.
Cut-Throat Grok:	*Grins* Lets see what you can brew!
```

## Extracted Facts

- Rak returned to Grok at night after earlier failing to stop by.
- Rak apologized for bailing earlier and explained that he lost track of time while cooking.
- The word "again" and Rak's apology imply prior contact that day or earlier.
- Grok was willing to open the shop for Rak.
- Grok remembered that Rak had mentioned alcohol.
- Rak said he needed ingredients and his kit to brew.
- Rak claimed he could brew almost anything with the kit and ingredients.
- Rak used the phrase / table joke, "I put on my robe and wizard hat."
- Grok responded positively and invited him to show what he could brew.
- GM reconstruction presumes Grok furnished Rak's alchemy kit and ingredients.
- This scene is the first recovered explicit Rak/Grok friendship-development beat, eventually leading into their later intimate relationship.

## Reconstruction Use

Place this on the night of Erastus 6 after the workday / Bloody Hour / dinner structure unless later logs place it more specifically.
