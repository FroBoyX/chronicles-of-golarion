# Wormwood Shipboard Tasks

## Document Status

- Type: Evidence / AP scaffold extraction
- Maturity: Structured extract
- Source: GM-provided AP task text
- Scope: Rigger, swab, and cook's mate daily work tasks aboard the Wormwood
- Canon status: Published/AP baseline scaffold unless table-specific events contradict it

## Rigger Tasks

| d6 | Task | Requirements / Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | Rigging Repair | Repair damaged rigging. Requires DC 10 Climb checks to reach rigging 30 feet up, then DC 10 Profession (sailor) or Dexterity check. |
| 2 | Line Work | Hoist and lower sails. Requires DC 10 Profession (sailor) or Dexterity check. Also DC 10 Constitution check to avoid fatigue at end of shift. |
| 3 | Upper Rigging Work | Work in upper rigging 50 feet up. Requires DC 10 Climb checks, then DC 10 Profession (sailor) or Dexterity check. |
| 4 | Rope Work | Coil, stow, and secure ropes to cleats and bollards. Requires DC 10 Profession (sailor) or Dexterity check. |
| 5 | Lookout | Climb to crow's nest 60 feet up. Requires DC 10 Climb checks, then DC 10 Perception check. |
| 6 | Mainsail Duties | Raise and lower the mainsail. Requires DC 10 Profession (sailor) or Strength check. Also DC 10 Constitution check to avoid fatigue at end of shift. |

## Swab Tasks

| d6 | Task | Requirements / Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | Man the Bilges | Vile, sweaty bilge cleaning. Requires DC 12 Strength check. Also DC 10 Constitution check to avoid fatigue at end of shift. |
| 2 | Rat Catcher | Catch rats and other vermin belowdecks. Requires DC 10 Stealth, Survival, or Dexterity check. |
| 3 | Swab the Decks | Mop decks and scrub them with holystones. Requires DC 10 Strength or Constitution check. Failing either check results in fatigue at end of shift. |
| 4 | Hauling Rope and Knot Work | Tie/untie knots and move heavy rope coils. Requires DC 10 Profession (sailor) or Strength check. Also DC 10 Constitution check to avoid fatigue at end of shift. |
| 5 | Runner | Pass messages around the ship except officers' cabins. Requires DC 10 Acrobatics and DC 10 Constitution check. Failing Constitution check causes fatigue. A PC with Run automatically succeeds. |
| 6 | Repairs | Sew sails, splice rope, and fix things that break. Requires DC 10 Profession (sailor) or Dexterity check. |

## Cook's Mate Tasks

| d6 | Task | Requirements / Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1-2 | Cooking | Assist Ambrose Kroop preparing the day's meal. If Kroop is sober, no check. If Kroop is drunk, DC 10 Profession (cook) or Intelligence check. |
| 3 | Fishing | Use ship's nets to catch supper. DC 10 Profession (fisherman) or Survival check. Failure results in a day in the bilges as punishment the following day. |
| 4 | Turtle Hunting | Hunt leatherback sea turtles with harpoons, hooks, and nets. DC 10 Profession (fisherman) or Survival check. Failure results in a day in the bilges as punishment the following day. |
| 5 | Bull Session | Drink with Ambrose Kroop and listen to his stories. Cook's mate must drink an additional rum ration, but can take an additional ship action during the day. |
| 6 | Special Occasion | Harrigan is celebrating and wants a pig butchered and cooked. DC 10 Profession (cook) or Survival check to slaughter animal, then help clean, cut, and prepare carcass. If Kroop is sober, no additional check. If Kroop is drunk, DC 15 Profession (cook) or Intelligence check. |
