## Paraphrased Scaffold

As the Wormwood approaches the Slithering Coast, the water becomes shallow enough that navigation is difficult.

In the afternoon, Plugg gathers the PCs on the main deck with crab pots at his feet. He says Harrigan wants fresh crab for supper, points to a nearby reef, and orders the PCs plus one friendly NPC to swim out, fill the pots, and return.

The water is calm and clear enough that swimming and visibility are manageable. The PCs can search the reef for crabs, swim down, and catch them. Four ordinary crabs fill one pot.

Two reefclaws live in the reef. They emerge from the coral after the group has reached the reef and attack. They are determined predators and fight to the death.

A reefclaw can be used as food and counts as enough to fill one crab pot.

If the crew brings back reefclaw meat for Harrigan's supper, Harrigan rewards the successful character by returning all of one PC's confiscated equipment. If that character's equipment is already recovered, he rewards the group with a potion of cure moderate wounds instead.

## Table-Relevant Points

- Plugg uses Harrigan's desire for fresh crab as the stated reason for the dangerous errand.
- The AP default task includes PCs plus one friendly NPC.
- Table evidence for this run identifies the selected group as Harvus, Rak, Sho, Mirei, and Badger.
- Table evidence says Rak brought back the big reefclaw and was brought before Harrigan.
- Harrigan rewarded Rak by instructing Grok to return anything taken from him from ship stores.
