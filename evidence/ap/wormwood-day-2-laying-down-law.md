## Encounter Scaffold

The AP places the Day 2 encounter at dawn, immediately after the ship bell calls the crew onto deck.

The scene begins in the crew berths. Four lower-crew pirates block the new hands' route to deck:

- Aretta Bansion
- Fipps Chumlett
- Jaundiced Jape
- Slippery Syl Lonegan

Master Scourge has already spoken to the four pirates and encouraged them to rough up the new swabs. The goal is to delay the new hands, give them a beating, and make them late to their shift.

The encounter is intended as a fistfight rather than a lethal fight. The pirates begin without weapons drawn. Other sailors linger nearby to watch.

## Punishment Logic

Anyone who arrives late to deck, PC or NPC, can be punished with lashes for laziness.

If the conflict escalates to murder, Plugg investigates and punishes the murderer severely.

If weapons are drawn, the pirates generally back down, with Fipps as the most likely to escalate before retreating if faced with enough armed resistance.

## Table Reconstruction Use

Use this AP scaffold only for the baseline structure:

- dawn timing;
- crew berths location;

The table-specific version overrides the generic outcome. In this campaign, the harassing group ended up unconscious and was whipped at Bloody Hour for being late.
