# Harvus Light Timeline Notes

## Document Status

- Type: Evidence / player note preservation
- Maturity: Raw-to-structured extraction
- Source: Harvus character light timeline notes provided by GM
- Canon status: Evidence, not final chronology until reconciled with logs and GM rulings

## Purpose

This document preserves Harvus' player-facing light timeline notes and extracts usable continuity from them.

The notes are informal, subjective, and partially typo-ridden. They are valuable because they show what Harvus' player considered memorable in the moment.

## Interpretation Rules

- Day labels are Harvus-note labels, not automatically canonical absolute dates.
- Spelling and phrasing reflect the original note style where useful.
- Character impressions are Harvus' perspective, not objective truth.
- Where the notes conflict with later GM truth, GM truth wins.
- Where the notes add first-person memory, they should be preserved even if later chronology is refined.

## Immediate Recognition On Waking

- Jaundiced Jape
- Aretta Bansion
- Maheem

This supports the existing truth that Harvus' press-ganging was not anonymous from his perspective.

## Initial PC Impressions

Harvus' notes record early impressions of the other PCs:

- Sho Astor: 6'2", wiry, short hair, pale-purple skin.
- Rak Wraithraiser: described as an alligator person, with a joking note about no bananas.
- Mirei: Aasimar, reduced in the note to physical impression.

These should be treated as Harvus/player shorthand rather than full characterization.

This confirms his primary Book One ship role.

## Rosie

- necklace,

Rosie's later loss in the storm therefore carries direct emotional weight for Harvus.

## Sandara Relationship Development

Harvus sought solace with Sandara after Rosie's loss and later drank with her.

The notes record:

- Sandara from Hell Harbor,
- poor family,
- grew up on the streets,
- turned to life at sea,
- drinking with Sandara led to severe drunkenness and wisdom damage,
- later a kiss / smooch with Sandara on the Man's Promise sequence.

This supports the existing Harvus/Sandara relationship arc.

## Scourge Escalation

Harvus' notes record a rage slip during boarding exercises / defensive combat training.

He insulted Scourge, was lashed, nearly avoided unconsciousness, was whipped again, and went down.

This reinforces Scourge as Harvus' personal antagonist and shows Harvus' rage was not fully controlled.

Later, Sho informed Harvus that Scourge tried to have her killed and that she had been boxed.

Harvus' note says the thought of Scourge's villainy made his blood boil and that he would see Scourge's blood spilled.
