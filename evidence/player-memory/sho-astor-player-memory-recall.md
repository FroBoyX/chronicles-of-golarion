## Extracted Memory Claims

### Early Wormwood / Day 1-2 Candidates

- Sho remembers standing up for someone on the first day. GM placement identifies this as Jakes Magpie and currently treats the consequence as a warning, not a whipping.
- Sho remembers later being whipped, possibly for speaking up for Rak or another act of defiance.
- Sho remembers being disguised as a man originally.
- Sho remembers Mirei noticing the disguise early, probably on the first or second day.
- Sho remembers confiding in Mirei about the disguise.
- Sho believes a later whipping made the disguise harder or impossible to conceal because of her cries.

### Gear Recovery

- Sho remembers getting her reversible cloak back.
- She paid more than was offered because the player misunderstood the gold-to-silver exchange rate.
- The source of the silver is unresolved because she had been drugged and stripped during press-ganging.
- Current memory: cloak and money are probably on the ship at the current reconstruction point.

### Rosie Storm Sequence

- Sho remembers tying herself to a rope and diving in after Rosie.
- Rosie did not survive.

### Bilges Ambush / Frame Attempt

- Sho remembers Slippery Syl, Jaundiced Jape, and another person trying to ambush and kill her in the bilges.
- Sho barely won.
- Sho remembers trying to get help to stabilize the attackers.
- Sho believes one of the attackers finished off another so the captain would attempt to keelhaul Sho.
- Sho suspects Plugg or Scourge ordered the attack because they were mutually hostile by then.

### Owlbear

- Sho remembers somewhat befriending a meatheaded man normally chained belowdeck.
- She fought him.
- The fight had a feinting mechanic that worked in Sho's favor.

### Peppery Longfarthing

- Sho remembers failing badly at flirting with Peppery.
- Peppery seemed mildly amused, but not interested in someone Sho reads in memory as young and naively stupid.

### Man's Promise Assault

- Sho remembers being given command of Harvus, Rak, Mirei, and Giffer Tibbs during the assault on the Man's Promise.
- Sho remembers calling out to Captain Harrigan in the fog to prevent him from being attacked from behind.
- Harrigan later used that warning as a reason to spare Sho from keelhauling.
- Harrigan gave Sho a locket or pendant of some kind.
- Sho's player knows Rak killed Giffer Tibbs in the assault, but Sho as a character found no evidence to support that after the battle.
- Mirei failed to climb the ropes and stayed aboard the Wormwood.
- Rak and Harvus failed to follow Sho's commands and killed people Sho had sworn would not be harmed if they laid down their weapons.

### Rum Ration

- Sho made a point of never drinking her rum rations.
- She remembers dumping them overboard or somewhere no one would look.
- She believes she was successful each time and was not caught.

### Current Man's Promise / Relaunch State

- Sho is confident she kept Harrigan's pendant/locket on her person.
- Sho carries major guilt for the crew of the Man's Promise.
- Sho has been trying to make right by them with little success.
- Sho believes this guilt/effort is why Man's Promise survivors may be about to let her out to help with the mutiny.
- Agatha Dreamriver remains a strongly remembered emotional anchor/crush.
