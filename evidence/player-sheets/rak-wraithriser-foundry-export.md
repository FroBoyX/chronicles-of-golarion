## Traits / Character Hooks

### Buccaneer's Blood

The sheet includes the Skull & Shackles campaign trait **Buccaneer's Blood**.

The trait text states that one of Rak's ancestors was an infamous Free Captain of the Shackles and that piracy is in his blood.

It also records the Formidably Maid setup: Rak went to the notorious pirate hangout in Port Peril, was distracted by a flirtatious corsair during a night of booze and romance, then was beaten senseless and stuffed in a sack.

This directly supports the existing Rak backstory.

### Axe to Grind

Rak has the **Axe to Grind** trait: "There is a fire in your heart that can't be quenched."

Mechanically, it gives bonus damage when only he threatens a foe.

Narratively, it supports Rak as someone suited to isolated violence.

### Omen

Rak has the **Omen** trait.

The trait text frames him as a harbinger of some future event and grants an ominous presence.

This may support Rak's pirate-legacy / unsettling-alchemist tone.

### Trustworthy

Rak has the **Trustworthy** trait.

This is notable because his build and behavior can be violent or ominous, but the sheet also mechanically supports social reliability or persuasive trust.
