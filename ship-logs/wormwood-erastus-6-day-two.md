## Log Entry

**Erastus 6, 4712 AR - Laying Down the Law**  
Weather normal.

Morning bell called hands to duty. New hands were delayed below by Slippery Syl Lonegan, Aretta Bansion, Jaundiced Jape, and Fipps Chumlett. The four were left unconscious in the lower hold before the new hands came on deck.

Rak Wraithraiser, Harvus G'ron, and Sho Astor reported the obstruction to Master Scourge. Scourge sent the new hands on to work and deferred punishment of the failed obstruction group until later.

Day's labor proceeded under standard Wormwood routine:

- Harvus G'ron continued rigger work.
- Conchobhar Turlach Shortstone continued rigger work.
- Mirei, Sho Astor, and Crimson Cogward continued swab labor.
- Rak Wraithraiser worked below as cook's mate under Ambrose Kroop.

During the workday, Mirei spoke with Tilly Brackett while laboring. Harvus spoke with Maheem in the rigging. Sho Astor approached Cut-Throat Grok at the quartermaster's store seeking recovery of personal property, but Grok did not trade gear for promises or future service.

At Bloody Hour, Slippery Syl Lonegan, Aretta Bansion, Jaundiced Jape, and Fipps Chumlett were whipped for lateness after failing to delay the new hands successfully.

Evening leisure followed below. Mirei played harp for nearby crew. Harvus and Rak praised the performance. Badger Medlar asked where Mirei learned to play. Jack Scrimshaw and Giffer Tibbs were present in the lower-hold leisure space.

Rak later returned to Grok, apologized for missing an earlier chance to meet, and discussed brewing alcohol. Grok opened access to ingredients and alchemy equipment.

Day closed with the new hands established as capable of fighting back, but not safe from future reprisals.
