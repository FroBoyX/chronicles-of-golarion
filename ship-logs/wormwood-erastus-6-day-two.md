## Log Entry

**Erastus 6, 4712 AR - Laying Down the Law**  
Weather normal.

Morning bell called hands to duty. Lower-crew disturbance reported in crew berths before shift. New hands were obstructed by Slippery Syl Lonegan, Aretta Bansion, Jaundiced Jape, and Fipps Chumlett. Obstruction failed. Four obstructing hands left unconscious below.

Rak Wraithraiser, Harvus G'ron, and Sho Astor reported the disturbance to Master Scourge. Report accepted for purposes of immediate duty. New hands sent on to work. Punishment of late hands deferred to Bloody Hour.

Duty assignments continued under standing arrangement:

- Harvus G'ron and Conchobhar Turlach Shortstone: riggers.
- Mirei, Sho Astor, and Crimson Cogward: swabs.
- Rak Wraithraiser: cook's mate under Ambrose Kroop.

Day's labor completed under standard routine. New hands remain insolent but useful. Sho Astor separately approached quartermaster's store seeking return of property. No trade recorded. Quartermaster retained goods.

At Bloody Hour, Slippery Syl Lonegan, Aretta Bansion, Jaundiced Jape, and Fipps Chumlett punished by lash for lateness and failure to report.

Evening meal and rum ration issued. Lower hold settled without further recorded violence. Mirei played harp during lower-crew leisure. No discipline issued for performance. Rak Wraithraiser later attended quartermaster's store regarding brewing materials. Quartermaster opened access at discretion.

Day closed with new hands marked capable of disorder and requiring continued watch.
