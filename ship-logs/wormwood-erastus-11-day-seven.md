## Log Entry

**Erastus 11, 4712 AR - Things Returned in the Rain**  
Heavy rain throughout the day, with conditions worsening toward night.

Routine assignments continued despite wet rigging, poor footing, and reduced visibility. Riggers and lower-deck hands remained at stations. No major injury, loss of labor, or significant damage recorded before close of watch.

Quartermaster stores released selected personal effects to Harvus G'ron: one ring, one necklace, and a fiddle belonging to Rosie Cusswell. The fiddle was returned to Cusswell during evening leisure.

Mirei approached the captain's cabin with an invitation for the evening performance. Mr. Plugg directed her below before the weather worsened and ordered the performance used to settle the lower crew.

Music continued in the middle hold during evening leisure. Crew remained contained below without disorder while rain strengthened above.

Weather continued to build at close of watch. Extra attention to lines, deck footing, and exposed stores recommended.
