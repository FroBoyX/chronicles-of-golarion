## Log Entry

**Erastus 12, 4712 AR - No Dry Place**  
Full storm. Horizon lost for most of the watch period.

All hands called before dawn. Day watch and night watch remained under continuous emergency assignment for more than twenty hours. Sailing Master Peppery Longfarthing directed ship handling from the poop deck under Captain Harrigan. Mr. Plugg and Master Scourge coordinated deck labor and work details.

Rigging, helm, bailing, cargo restraint, galley, stores, and repair details remained active throughout the day and night. Normal watch relief, meals, and rest periods were suspended or sharply reduced. Minor falls, strained joints, split hands, and storm-related bruising were treated as work allowed. No major structural loss recorded.

Rosie Cusswell went overboard during the night storm. Harvus G'ron raised the alarm from the rigging. Rak Wraithraiser made repeated line attempts. Cusswell caught the line but failed to retain it in the sea state.

Sho Astor entered the water secured to a second line and attempted to reach Cusswell while line recovery continued. A required change of course to meet an incoming sea pulled Astor away and left her being dragged astern. Rescue effort shifted to Astor before a second sailor was lost.

Astor was recovered alive by Wraithraiser, Mirei, Sandara Quinn, Maheem, and additional hands from both watches. She returned aboard bruised and having taken substantial seawater, but remained capable of duty after immediate recovery.

Cusswell disappeared from view and was not recovered. Status recorded as lost at sea. Death not confirmed.

Emergency labor resumed immediately. Storm conditions remained unbroken at close of entry. Both watches continued under standing storm orders.
