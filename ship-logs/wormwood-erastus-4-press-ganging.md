## Log Entry

**Erastus 4, 4712 AR — Port Peril**  
Wormwood at port. Shore collection detail active at and around the Formidably Maid.

New crew secured for intake:

- **Sho Astor** — acquired through Kipper's contact lure. Drugged, stripped of carried possessions, and delivered to the ship.
- **Mirei** — observed near the Formidably Maid's side ways after Sho's removal; followed by Slippery Syl Lonegan, struck near the water, searched, and delivered with Tam Tate assisting final transfer aboard.
- **Rak Wraithraiser** — acquired through Melina Drakesworn arrangement. Patch Patchsalt and Shivikah collected and bagged subject after Scourge's signal.
- **Conchobhar Turlach Shortstone** — marked useful for ship service and taken quietly amid tavern disorder. Exact collector not entered.
- **Harvus G'ron** — subdued after staged tavern disturbance and street ambush. Tam Tate, Maheem, Jaundiced Jape, and Aretta Bansion participated under Scourge's direction. Subject sustained left wing injury during collection.

**Crimson Cogward** entered separately as volunteer crew. Not listed among press-ganged captures.

Confiscated goods routed for quartermaster handling.

Collection complete before departure. Wormwood slipped Port Peril under cover of night with new hands secured below.
