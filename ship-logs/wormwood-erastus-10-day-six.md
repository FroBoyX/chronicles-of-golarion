**Erastus 10, 4712 AR - The Beast Plugg Made**  
Weather fair.

Routine duties completed under standing assignments. No significant loss of labor or stores recorded.

After the evening meal, Mr. Plugg conducted a supervised unarmed bout on the main deck between Sho and Owlbear Hartshorn. Rules limited both participants to fists and wrestling. Weapons and magic were prohibited.

Hartshorn held the advantage in size and strength. Sho used movement, changes of angle, and Hartshorn's impaired sight to avoid his heavier blows and control the engagement.

Sho gained the advantage and ceased pressing the bout once Hartshorn could no longer continue effectively. No serious injury or immediate loss of duty capacity recorded for either participant.

Hartshorn was returned below following the contest. Crew dispersed without further incident.

Sho demonstrated effective judgment, restraint, and tactical discipline before the assembled crew. Her conduct and Hartshorn's response drew notice. Continued observation recommended.
