## Log Entry

**Erastus 13, 4712 AR - What the Storm Left**  
Storm weakening. Seas remained rough through most of the day.

Emergency watches continued into the morning. Sailing Master Peppery Longfarthing released exhausted hands in stages while maintaining sufficient crew at the helm, rigging, pumps, and deck stations.

Damage inspection and recovery work completed throughout the ship. Standing water cleared from lower spaces. Rigging, canvas, knots, lashings, barrels, stores, galley fittings, and exposed seams inspected and restored where required. Spoiled or seawater-damaged stores separated from serviceable stock.

Habbly Quarne treated split hands, bruising, strained joints, and other minor storm injuries as work permitted. Sho Astor remained bruised from the prior night's recovery but continued assigned labor. Rak Wraithraiser returned to galley duty and assisted Ambrose Kroop in providing hot broth and preserved bread to recovering hands.

Rosie Cusswell remains missing following the prior night's overboard incident. No sign observed astern and no recovery made. Status remains lost at sea; death not confirmed.

Normal watch divisions resumed during the afternoon. Crew morale remained subdued, but no disorder was recorded. Routine assignments and shipboard discipline were restored before close of watch.
