## Log Entry

**Erastus 7, 4712 AR - Wormwood Day 3**  
Weather normal.

Morning bell called hands to duty. No weather interruption recorded. New hands reported to assigned labor under standing arrangement.

Day's work proceeded under standard routine:

- Harvus G'ron and Conchobhar Turlach Shortstone: riggers.
- Mirei, Sho Astor, and Crimson Cogward: swabs.
- Rak Wraithraiser: cook's mate under Ambrose Kroop.

No unusual Bloody Hour entry recorded for this date.

Quartermaster reported continued request from Sho Astor for return of personal cloak. No trade accepted. Goods retained pending item of value.

Evening meal and lower-crew leisure proceeded without recorded violence. Sandara Quinn observed speaking with Harvus G'ron during meal. Mirei performed harp below. No discipline issued.

Rak Wraithraiser attended quartermaster's store again after hours. Quartermaster reports new hand seeking advice regarding hostile crew and officers. New hand warned to avoid trouble and report to bilges duty on next shift.

New hands remain unsettled but useful. Scourge's attention to be maintained, particularly concerning Sho Astor and Rak Wraithraiser.
