## Log Entry

**Erastus 9, 4712 AR - Something in the Bilges**  
Weather fair above deck. Excessive heat recorded belowdecks.

Routine labor proceeded under standing assignments.

Jack Scrimshaw's prior report of a bite suffered while rat-catching in the bilges was reviewed after continued concern over vermin below. Investigation detail assigned under Mr. Plugg's authority. Rak, Sho, and Harvus were ordered below and instructed not to return until the hazard was destroyed.

Detail found six oversized rats nesting among spoiled barley in the bilge water. Confined space, heat, poor footing, and concealment in the water complicated removal.

All six rats destroyed. Rak, Sho, and Harvus returned with bites, minor wounds, and substantial bilge fouling. No loss of life or immediate loss of duty capacity recorded.

Bilges returned to serviceable condition. Spoiled barley remains a stores concern. No further vermin movement reported before close of watch.

Rak, Sho, and Harvus completed assigned work despite recent discipline concerns. Continued observation recommended. Their cooperation under hazardous conditions was effective.
